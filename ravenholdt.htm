•  
    Ravenholdt Manor
    From level 30, when killing Syndicate Spies in Alterac Mountains, the player might loot a Shady Missive, which begins a new quest that requires finding some items in the buildings of Strahnbrad and taking them to Ravenholdt Manor. Completing this quest unlocks four subsequent quests at levels 30, 35, 40, and 60. These quests will require the player to travel to remote places and either kill the occupant(s) or burglarise the building for important documents. The first three of these quests are started by Ian Strom in Booty Bay, now revealed to be an agent of Ravenholdt. The level 60 quest is called Mission: Karazhan and only becomes available when the player is Honoured and has completed the other three quests.

    Rogues are still introduced to the faction earlier via their level 24 quest The Manor, Ravenholdt; but they will still need to complete the Shady Missive quest to gain access to these new quests.

    There is also a new level 60 quest chain for Rogues called Mission: Relic Hunt; this is started by Fahrad when the player is Revered and able to craft Spiteful Poison IV.

    Faction reputation can be farmed using an Informant's Log. The repeatable quest Junkboxes Needed can also be used to farm reputation.

    The faction quartermaster sells new bind-on-pickup items, including five types of thrown weapon, three trinkets, four Rogue handbooks, and two crafting recipes:

    • Thrown Weapons: Poisoned Throwing Knives, Chilled Throwing Knives, Poisoned Throwing Blades, Chilled Throwing Axes, and Darkened Throwing Axes.
    • Trinkets: Lesser Mark of Ravenholdt, Mark of Ravenholdt, and Seal of Raveholdt.
    • Rogue: Handbook of Spite Powder (Friendly, Rogue), Handbook of Spiteful Poison II (Friendly, Rogue), Handbook of Spiteful Poison III (Honoured, Rogue), and Handbook of Spiteful Poison IV (Revered, Rogue).
    • Cooking: Recipe: Shimmering Broth (Honoured, Rogue).
    • Leatherworking: Pattern: Enhancement Kit - Superior Dexterity (Revered).

  •  
    Informant's Log
    Binds when picked up. Unique.
    An Informant's Log can be obtained for free by any character who has completed the Shady Missive quest by talking to Myrokos Silentform. This item allows the player to collect reconnaissance as an agent of Ravenholdt Manor. While you have this item in your inventory, when killing or pickpocketing humanoids that award (or would award) experience you have a small chance to loot items that can be handed-in to increase your reputation with Ravenholdt (similar to Scourgestones for the Ardent Dawn):
    • Illegible Document (junk).
    • Useful Document (10 reputation).
    • Fencing Operation (10 reputation).
    • Important Coordinates (10 reputation).
    • Hand-drawn Map (50 reputation).
    • Sabotage Plans (200 reputation).
    • Secret Missive (200 reputation).

    All of these items are bind-on-pickup, stack up to 250, and have no sell value. Each kill can yield up to 1 of these items; each pickpocket can separately yield up to 1, so a Rogue can obtain up to 2 from each humanoid. The junk and white quality items have a drop rate of 4% each, the greens 1%, and the blues ~0.1% each. They can be handed-in either to Myrokos Silentform in Ravenholdt Manor, to Ian Strom in Booty Bay, to Arantir in Lakeshire (Alliance only), or to a new agent in the Violet Eye camp in Deadwind Pass.

  •  
    Mission: Karazhan
    New raid quest; last in a chain (see above). The quest giver is located in the Violet Eye camp in Deadwind Pass. Requires the player to find 20 documents scattered throughout Karazhan Tower. These documents are found in interactable book objects hidden in the dungeon, similar to the Important Blackrock Documents in LBRS. There are hundreds of possible locations in the dungeon where they could spawn and each raid instance will randomly spawn 4-7 of them (quest completion will take multiple runs). Everyone in the raid who is on the quest can loot the book objects. Looting a book object will bind the player to the raid instance (similar to killing a boss). Completing the quest will reward the Suspicious Pot Plant trinket.

    Mission: Relic Hunt
    New Rogue quest chain. Fahrad requires 20 Spiteful Poison IVs and will then send the player to bring him relics from Dire Maul: East, Scholomance, and Karazhan Crypts. He will give the bundle of relics back to the player and send him to a cave in the Alterac Mountains to trade it with a dealer. The cave is empty except for an old cart; interacting with it will trigger the dealer and 3 bodyguards to spawn and attack the player. They are level 58 and ignore stealth. When the dealer is killed, the player brings his head to Fahrad and explains the betrayal. Completing the chain rewards Handbook: Spiteful Poison V and the Scorpid's Bite ring.
