•  
    Honour Quests
    To encourage players to participate in all three battlegrounds, the rewards for the Concerted Efforts and For Great Honor quests (both the initial and repeatable versions) have been improved.

    The honour given by these quests has been increased by 10% from 2388 to 2627.

    These quests now also award a choice of 1 Major Mana Potion or 1 Moist Towelette.

  •  
    Battleground Emissaries
    For convenience (and to make cities feel alive), the patrolling emissaries for all battlegrounds will now walk around the capital cities all the time, not just during their holiday weekends. (The stationary emissaries and banners still only appear during their own holiday.)
  •  
    Preparation (Buff)
    To give a short grace period to casters for buffing, the Preparation buff (which reduces the mana cost of all spells to 0) is now cast on players for 3 seconds whenever they are respawned at a graveyard and whenever they join a game after it has already started.
  •  
    Portals
    To avoid grieving, portal spells cannot be cast in battlegrounds. This includes Mage portal spells and the Portal: Karazhan spell cast with Atiesh.
  •  
    Spawn Portals
    Players can once again walk through the spawn portal inside a battleground to exit the game. To avoid grieving, all fear, mind control, and polymorph effects are instantly removed from players within 10 yards of a spawn portal.
  •  
    Deserter (Debuff)
    The Deserter debuff still prevents the player from joining any battleground and persists through death.

    If it is applied as a result of the player exiting the game through the spawn portal, disconnecting, or typing '/afk', then it will last for 15 minutes.

    However, if it is applied as a result of actually being AFK in a battleground for 5 minutes or being reported as inactive by other players and not refuting it in the grace period, then:

    • it lasts for 1 hour;
    • all honour obtained by the player in that battleground will be subtracted;
    • the player's reputation with the battleground's PVP faction will be reduced by 1000 (to a minimum of 1/3000 Neutral) (if the player loses a reputation level, any owned equipment that requires a higher reputation cannot be used and will be de-equipped and put into the inventory, or mailed if there's no room); and
    • if a player receives this 1 hour version 3 times in a single week, the third one will last until the end of the week, preventing the player from joining any more battlegrounds for the rest of that week.

    If a player is reported as inactive by 40% of other players, he will receive a 5 minute grace period within which he must engage in PVP (either by fighting in combat or interacting with an objective) before being kicked and receiving the 1 hour debuff. This period is still indicated by a temporary debuff, but it will no longer trigger any indicator outside of the game (such as by flashing the Windows taskbar).

    You can no longer report a player as inactive until at least 30 seconds after the game has started.

  •  
    Alterac Valley Holiday Bonus Honour
    The amount of holiday bonus honour given at the end of each Alterac Valley game during the Alterac Valley holiday has been halved.
  •  
    Elemental Lords
    To prevent abuse, Ivus the Forest Lord and Lokholar the Ice Lord will reset if agro'ed by a player who is inside an entrance cave; they will not enter the cave and the elite guards at the entrance cave will not attack them.
  •  
    Ground and Cavalry Assaults
    To reflect the shorter duration of typical games of Alterac Valley, the amount of Irondeep/Coldtooth Supplies required to trigger a ground assault has been reduced from 280 to 120 for the near faction and from 70 to 40 for the far faction. The amount of tamed animals and hides required for a cavalry assault has been reduced from 25 to 10. Each type of assault now triggers its own 30 minute cooldown before it can be issued again; after 20 minutes of this cooldown have passed, the repeatable hand-in quests will be completable again in preparation for the next.
  •  
    Bottled Alterac Spring Water
    The cost of Bottled Alterac Spring Water has increased to 60 silver (per 5), consistent with Blessed Sunfruit Juice.
