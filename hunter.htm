Primary Goals:
  • Tidy the Marksmanship tree and remove bloat.
  • Make Scorpid Sting a viable option for group play.
  • Buff the Survival tree to give more meaningful options, with a focus on solo play and PvP.
Beast Mastery
  • Improved Eyes of the Beast (removed; was used to pull bosses in MC in the early days)
  • Focused Fire (passive damage [from TBC])
  • Improved Aspect of the Monkey (3 ranks)
  • Pathfinding (includes Aspect of the Beast)
  • Spirit Bond (better value per point)
  • Bestial Discipline (3 ranks)
Marksmanship
  • Improved Arcane Shot (2 ranks; available for low level players)
  • Improved Hunter's Mark (3 ranks)
  • Rapid Killing (reduces cooldown of Rapid Fire [from TBC])
  • Improved Stings (renamed from Improved Serpent Sting; more damage and resistance to dispel effects [from TBC])
  • Improved Scorpid Sting (removed; unpopular)
  • Careful Aim (increases ranged attack power [from TBC] and Arcane Shot damage)
Survival
  • Improved Raptor Strike (increases damage)
  • Monster Slaying (available later)
  • Improved Wing Clip (3 ranks; increased proc chance to discourage spam but added a cooldown)
  • Savage Strikes (includes Counterattack)
  • Propitiousness (Raptor Strike crits when target is stuck)
  • Trap Mastery (reduces cooldown)
  • Resourcefulness (reduces cost of traps and melee abilities [from TBC])
  • Counterattack (increased base damage)
  • Wyvern Sting (added slow effect)
Modified Items
  •  
    Striker's Set
    The Striker's (T2.5 set) 5-piece set bonus now increases your ranged attack power by 100 when under the effect of Rapid Fire, instead of reducing its cooldown.
  •  
    Guide: Tranquilizing Shot
    The Tome of Tranquilizing Shot has been renamed to Guide: Tranquilizing Shot, consistent with other Hunter books.
Modified Services
  •  
    Stable Master
    The number of pets that can be stabled has been increased from 2 to 4. However, only the first stable slot is free; the others must be unlocked for a fee, similar to bag slots in the bank. The second costs 1 gold to unlock. The third requires level 40 and costs 10 gold. The fourth requires level 60 and costs 100 gold.
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Feed Pet
    Feed Pet now applies to the pet any Well Fed bonus that the food may give, just as it would to a player, as long as the pet is allowed to eat without attacking or being attacked for the required amount of time.

    In addition, Feed Pet can now be used while mounted without dismounting the Hunter.

  •  
    Scorpid Sting
    Cast: instant. Cost: Z mana.
    Stings the target, reducing Strength and Agility by X and chance to hit with melee and ranged attacks by Y% for 20 seconds. Only one sting per Hunter can be active on any one target. (Requires Ranged Weapon.)

    [Rank 1: X = 20, Y = 1, Z = 70   (level 22),
     Rank 2: X = 29, Y = 1, Z = 90   (level 32),
     Rank 3: X = 45, Y = 1, Z = 125 (level 42),
     Rank 4: X = 68, Y = 2, Z = 165 (level 52).
    Ranks 1-4 are learned from trainer.]

New Abilities
  •  
    Kill Command
    Cast: instant. Range: 100 yards.
    Cost: 70 mana.
    Give the command to kill, causing your pet to instantly attack for an additional 65 damage. Can only be used after the Hunter lands a critical strike on the target. (Cooldown: 20 seconds.) [From TBC.]

    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]

  •  
    Raptor Strike (Rank 9)
    Cast: instant. Cost: 110 mana.
    A strong attack that increases melee damage by 165. (Cooldown: 6 seconds.)

    [Rank 9, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
