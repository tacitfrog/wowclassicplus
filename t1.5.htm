    Tier 1.5 Set
    New 5-piece tier set for each class. These sets have item levels 66-70 and contain the following pieces: head, shoulders, chest, legs, and ring. They cater to specs that are usually not provisioned, such as Rogue tank, Shadow Priest, and Feral Druid. Set bonuses:
    Hunter:
    3: Increases the damage done by your stings by 10%.
    5: Increases the maximum focus of your pets by 20.
    Mage:
    3: All damage you take while channeling Evocation is reduced by 90%.
    5: Arcane Missiles critical strikes invigorate you for 45-95 health and mana ('Arcane Invigoration' effect).
    Priest:
    3: Increases the duration of your Shadow Word: Pain spell by 3 seconds.
    5: Mind Blast critical strikes increase the damage done by your next Mind Flay spell cast on the same target by 20% ('Weakened Mind' effect; lasts 10 seconds; lost if you change target).
    Rogue:
    3: When you dodge an attack, you regain 2 energy ('Evasive Retort' effect; cannot occur more than once per second).
    5: When you suffer a critical strike, the chance you'll be critically struck again by any attack is reduced by 90% for 8 seconds ('Lurk in the Shadows' effect; cannot occur more than once per minute).

  • Shaman:
    3: When you block an attack, you recover 17 health and mana ('Ancestral Protection' effect; cannot occur more than once per second).
    5: Reduces the mana cost of your totems by 15%.
    Warlock:
    3: Reduces the mana cost of your curses by 10%.
    5: Immolate periodic damage has a 6% chance to make your next Searing Pain spell instant cast ('Firestorm' effect; lasts 10 seconds).

    Tier 1.5 Acquisition
    T1.5 pieces drop from bosses in Karazhan Tower and Desolate Valley:
    • Head: dropped by Prince Malchezaar.
    • Shoulders: dropped by The Curator.
    • Chest: dropped by Lord Kazzak.
    • Legs: dropped by Netherspite.

    Two class-specific pieces drop per boss kill.

    Ring: class-specific bind-on-pickup gems drop from the Chess and Opera events (1 per event, random classes) in Karazhan Tower. The gem starts a quest chain with the Violet Eye's jewelcrafter. After taking the gem to the jewelcrafter, the player will need to reach Friendly with the faction to progress further. When Friendly, the jewelcrafter will show an interest in helping the player to craft the ring; he will need some materials and sends the player to find his apprentice. The player will find the apprentice's corpse in Karazhan Crypts. Investigating the corpse will trigger an event where cultists ambush the group; looting these cultists will give a note that indicates the presence of a cult hideout deep in the hills surrounding Deadwind Pass. The jewelcrafter will send the player to cleanse the cult and recover the apprentice's stolen keepsake from its leader. Finally, in gratitude for returning the keepsake, the jewelcrafter will send the player to gather the materials needed for the ring: some Glowing Sap from Plaguewood (Eastern Plaguelands), a Nightmare Clasp (see under Blacksmithing), 3 Mysterious Runes, and the gem. He will then craft the ring for the player.
