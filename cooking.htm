    Ghost Broth
    Requires level 50 and Rogue. Use: Increases chance to dodge by 3% for 30 minutes. (Does not stack with other food buffs.) Requires 1 Mountain Silversage, 1 Ghost Mushroom, and 1 Hyjal Nectar (see under Karazhan Crypts). Ghost Broths are non-binding, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. The recipe requires 275 skill, is bind-on-pickup, is Rogue only, and is sold by Ravenholdt when Honoured.
