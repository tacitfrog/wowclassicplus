Dual Specs
  •  
    Second Talent Spec
    At level 40, each character gets a second talent spec. The character will then always have one active spec that is currently applied and one inactive spec that currently has no effect.

    Characters can switch between specs by using their Focus Lodestone.

    When a character first reaches level 40, the new spec appears as an empty inactive spec.

    When a character pays to unlearn and relearn talents, only the active spec is modified.

  •  
    Focus Lodestone
    Binds when picked up. Unique.
    A Focus Lodestone can be obtained for free by any character at level 40 or higher by talking to an appropriate class trainer. It can only be used while out of combat and under the effect of Rested (in a friendly/neutral inn or capital city); it has a 10 second cast and a 1 hour cooldown. When used, it switches the character's specs so that the inactive spec becomes active.

    [Note: This item is deliberately similar to a Hearthstone, but for talents.]

Mail
  •  
    Enchanted Mail
    Enchanters can now send enchantments by mail. See under Enchanting.
  •  
    Express Mail
    Express Mail is a mailbox service available to all players. By checking the new 'Express Mail' checkbox on a regular mail, it will arrive in the recipient's mailbox within 1 minute of being sent (rather than the usual 1 hour). The fee for sending Express Mail is 3 silver plus 3 silver per additional attachment (rather than the usual 30 copper). Cash-on-delivery can be used as usual with Express Mail.
Storage
  •  
    Bank Space
    The player bank is now a 7x4 grid, providing a total base capacity of 28 spaces (up from 6x4=24) [from TBC]. The bank now also offers a seventh purchasable bag slot that can be unlocked at a cost of 250 gold.
  •  
    Mining Sacks
    Bags for mining materials have been added. See under Leatherworking.
