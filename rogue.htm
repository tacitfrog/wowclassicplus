New Abilities:
  •  
    Tease
    (Instant cast. Cost: 20 energy.)
    Teases the target, causing no damage but increasing your threat by a large amount, making the enemy more likely to attack you.
  •  
    Blade Dance
    (Instant cast. Cost: 25 energy.)
    Finishing move that increases your Parry chance and initially causes threat to all nearby enemies, making them more likely to attack you. Lasts longer, causes more threat, and grants more Parry chance per combo point:
    • 1 point: 14 seconds, 6 / 7 / 8% Parry
    • 2 points: 18 seconds, 7 / 9 / 11% Parry
    • 3 points: 22 seconds, 8 / 11 / 14% Parry
    • 4 points: 26 seconds, 9 / 13 / 17% Parry
    • 5 points: 30 seconds, 10 / 15 / 20% Parry
    (Rank 1 / 2 / 3, trainable at level 32 / 44 / 56.)
  •  
    Envenom
    (Instant cast. Cost: 35 energy.)
    Finishing move that deals instant poison damage based on your Deadly Poison doses on the target, increased by Attack Power. Following the Envenom attack your chance to apply Instant Poison is increased by 60% for 1 second plus an additional 1 second per combo point. One dose is activated per combo point:
    • 1 dose: 204 damage
    • 2 doses: 376 damage
    • 3 doses: 548 damage
    • 4 doses: 720 damage
    • 5 doses: 892 damage
    (Rank 1, trainable at level 58.)
