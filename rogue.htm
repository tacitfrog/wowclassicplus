Primary Goals:
  • Make viable tanking builds that enable a Rogue to tank in 5-man dungeons from level 30; these builds should be centred in Combat and have viable complements in each of the other trees.
  • Add a tanking poison that does not run out of charges (just like Crippling Poison). [Spiteful Poison.]
  • Add mechanisms to grab AOE agro (since AOE is intentionally hard with the combo point system). [Blade Dance, Spitebomb.]
  • Add passive bleed synergy to Hemorrhage for both PvP and bleed tank option.
  • Give thrown weapons some unique perks to balance out the lack of stats. [Poisons, Deadly Throw.]
Assassination
  • Improved Expose Armor & Murder (swapped positions; Murder 3 ranks)
  • Improved Backstab & Improved Slice and Dice (swapped positions [from TBC])
  • Vile Poisons (also applies to Spitebombs)
  • Crippling Brew (Other poisons and spitebombs also apply Crippling Poison [from WOTLK])
  • Vigor (regains energy, requires 5/5 Seal Fate)
Combat
  • Improved Sprint (available earlier)
  • Endurance (-1 minute per point)
  • Deadened Nerves (passive defence)
  • Dagger Specialization & Fist Weapon Specialization (merged [from WOTLK])
  • Blade Dance (tanking finishing move [from SOD], periodic AOE threat)
Subtlety
  • Master of Deception (reduces chance to be crit)
  • Sleight of Hand (removed; unpopular)
  • Initiative (available earlier)
  • Elusiveness (-1 minute per point)
  • Sadistic Smile (extra threat for bleed tanking)
  • Improved Sap (100% reliable [from TBC]; available earlier for low level dungeons)
  • Heightened Senses (available earlier)
  • Cheat Death ([from TBC]; requires a defensive ability to be active, so not just a freebie; helps mitigate randomness in avoidance tanking)
  • Hemorrhage (stronger bonus but fewer charges [from TBC], refreshes bleeds on hit; added extra rank)
  • Premeditation (increases damage of next attack on the target [from Shadowstep, TBC], increased duration)
New Abilities
  •  
    Deadly Throw
    Cast: instant. Range: 30 yards.
    Cost: 35 energy.
    Finishing move that reduces the movement of the target by 50% for 6 seconds and causes damage based on your thrown weapon, attack power, and number of combo points used. (Requires Thrown Weapon.) [From TBC.]

    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]

Modified Abilities
  •  
    Poisons
    Poisons can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.

    The Crippling Poison (Rank 2) icon has been fixed and the Wound Poison icon has been changed (to the Gromsblood icon) [from TBC].

  •  
    Throw
    The poison applied to the offhand weapon now also counts as being applied to any thrown weapon that the Rogue uses. The Throw and Deadly Throw abilities benefit from all active poison-enhancing bonuses.
  •  
    Wound Poison
    The effectiveness of Wound Poison has been increased by 20%: Rank 1/2/3/4 now reduces healing taken by 66/90/126/162 per dose.
