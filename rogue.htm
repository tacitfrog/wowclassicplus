Primary Goals:
  • Make viable tanking builds to enable a Rogue to tank in 5-man dungeons from level 30.
  • Add a tanking finishing move that increases survivability. [Blade Dance.]
  • Add a tanking poison that does not run out of charges (similar to Crippling Poison). [Spiteful Poison.]
  • Add mechanisms to grab AOE agro (since AOE is deliberately hard in the combo point system). [Tease.]
  • Make heavy Subtlety builds viable and functionally unique.
  • Add passive bleed synergy to Hemorrhage/Backstab. [Deadliness.]
  • Give thrown weapons some Rogue-specific perks to balance out the lack of stats. [Poisons, Deadly Throw.]
Assassination
  • Remorseless Attacks (does not require killing blow)
  • Improved Backstab (moved from Combat tree [from TBC])
  • Lethality (includes Riposte)
  • Deadened Nerves (passive defence)
  • Crippling Brew (other poisons also apply Crippling Poison [from WOTLK])
  • Painful Infusion (improves Spiteful and Wound Poisons)
  • Vigor (regains energy, requires 5/5 Seal Fate)
Combat
  • Combat Acumen (renamed from Improved Slice and Dice; moved from Assassination tree [from TBC]; includes Blade Dance)
  • Endurance (-1 minute per point)
  • Riposte (possible combo point)
  • Dagger Specialization & Fist Weapon Specialization (merged [from WOTLK])
  • Blade Dance (tanking finishing move [from SOD], increases threat generation)
  • Blade Flurry (poisons apply)
Subtlety
  • Master of Deception (added Distract)
  • Elusiveness (-1 minute per point)
  • Initiative (available earlier)
  • Improved Sap (100% reliable [from TBC]; available earlier for low level dungeons)
  • Serrated Blades (available earlier [from WOTLK])
  • Heightened Senses (available earlier)
  • Improved Ambush (2 ranks [from WOTLK])
  • Cheat Death ([from TBC]; requires a defensive ability to be active, so not just a freebie; helps mitigate randomness in avoidance tanking)
  • Hemorrhage (110%, stronger bonus but fewer charges [from TBC]; added extra rank)
  • Deadliness (refreshes bleeds, increases damage against bleeding targets)
  • Premeditation (increases damage of next attack on the target [from Shadowstep, TBC], increased duration)
Modified Items
  •  
    Bonescythe Set
    The Bonescythe (T3 set) 4-piece set bonus now affects all abilities that add combo points and regains 3 energy, down from 5. The 6-piece set bonus now reduces the threat caused by your Eviscerate and Rupture by 15%, instead of its old effect. The 8-piece set bonus proc now lasts 20 seconds and affects your next Sinister Strike, Backstab, Ghostly Strike, or Ambush.
  •  
    Venomous Totem
    The cooldown of Venomous Totem has been reduced from 5 minutes to 3 minutes.
  •  
    Handbook of Eviscerate IX
    The Manual of Eviscerate IX has had its name and icon changed, consistent with other Rogue books.
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Poisons
    Poisons can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.

    Wound Poison has been buffed. Wound Poison and Crippling Poison (Rank 2) have had their icons changed [from TBC]. See under Poisons.

    The poison applied to the offhand weapon now also counts as being applied to any thrown weapon that the Rogue uses. The Throw and Deadly Throw abilities benefit from all poison-enhancing bonuses. Any poison procs from thrown weapons will decrement the charge count of the offhand poison.

  •  
    Throw
    Throw now toggles autoshoot mode.

    Deadly Throw

    Cost: 25 energy.
    Finishing move that reduces the movement speed of the target by 50% for 1 second plus 1 additional second per combo point and causes 150% thrown weapon damage. (Requires Thrown Weapon.) [From TBC, but cheaper and duration depends on combo points.]
