Primary Goals:
  • Make heavy Assassination/poisons builds viable without adding Mutilate (daggers should be 'from behind' in Classic).
  • Make multiple viable Combat/Subtlety options to enable a Rogue to tank in 5-man dungeons from level 30.
Assassination
  • Improved Backstab & Improved Slice and Dice (swapped positions [from TBC])
  • Vile Poisons & Cold Blood (includes Envenom)
  • Searing Concoctions (raid buff [from WOTLK] to justify Envenom taking up a debuff slot)
  • Noxious Brew (buff to poison builds)
  • Vigor (regains energy [from Bonescythe 4-piece set bonus], requires 5/5 Seal Fate)
Combat
  • Improved Sprint (available earlier)
  • Nerves of Steel (passive defence, enables the Rogue to achieve 20% damage reduction if tanking with Blade Dance)
  • Dagger Specialization & Fist Weapon Specialization (merged [from WOTLK])
  • Blade Dance (tanking finishing move)
Subtlety
  • Sleight of Hand (removed)
  • Initiative (available earlier)
  • Sadistic Smile (extra threat for tanking)
  • Improved Sap (100% reliable [from TBC], available earlier for low level dungeons)
  • Heightened Senses (3 ranks, includes melee)
  • Cheat Death ([from TBC]; requires a defensive ability to be active, so not just a freebie; helps mitigate randomness in avoidance tanking)
  • Hemorrhage (stronger bonus but fewer charges [from TBC], refreshes bleeds on hit to enable a bleed tank option)
  • Premeditation (added free Distract on target)
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Poisons
    Poisons can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.
  •  
    Throw
    The poison applied to the off-hand weapon also counts as being applied to any thrown weapons that the Rogue uses. The Throw ability benefits from all active poison-enhancing talents and bonuses that are affecting the Rogue.
  •  
    Slice and Dice
    Slice and Dice is now categorised as a Combat ability (and so is Blade Dance).
New Abilities
  •  
    Shiv
    Cast: instant. Cost: 10 energy.
    An instant off-hand attack that deals 50% off-hand weapon damage and applies the off-hand poison to the target. (Cooldown: 10 seconds.)
    [Note: Does not award a combo point. Dual Wield Specialization increases the damage of this ability; 5/5 deals 100% weapon damage.]
    [Rank 1, trainable at level 44.]
  •  
    Corrosive Poison
    Reagents: [unspecified]
    Each strike has a 30% chance of poisoning the enemy, reducing their armour by X for 15 seconds and causing a large amount of threat. Stacks up to 5 times.
    [Note: Does not stack with Expose Armor or Sunder Armor. Similar to Crippling Poison, lasts 30 minutes and has unlimited charges.]
    [Rank 1: X = 200 (level 30),
     Rank 2: X = 300 (level 42),
     Rank 3: X = 400 (level 54),
     Rank 4: X = 450 (level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower).]
  •  
    Envenom
    Cast: instant. Cost: 35 energy.
    Finishing move that deals Nature damage based on your Deadly Poison or Wound Poison doses on the target, increased by Attack Power. Following the Envenom attack your chance to apply Instant Poison is increased by 60% for 1 second plus an additional 1 second per combo point. One dose is activated per combo point:
      1 dose:   204 damage
      2 doses: 376 damage
      3 doses: 548 damage
      4 doses: 720 damage
      5 doses: 892 damage
    [Note: If you have applied both types of poison to your target, a combination of both can be consumed by a single Envenom; Deadly Poison doses will always be consumed first.]
    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
