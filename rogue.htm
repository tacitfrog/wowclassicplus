Primary Goals:
  • Slightly buff Assassination/poisons builds; improve viability without adding Mutilate.
  • Make viable Combat/Subtlety tanking builds that enable a Rogue to tank in 5-man dungeons from level 30.
  • Add a tanking poison that does not run out of charges (like Crippling Poison).
  • Add a mechanism to overcome bad proc luck to enable reliable late-game tanking (Shiv).
  • Accommodate bleed tanking builds using Hemorrhage.
Assassination
  • Improved Backstab & Improved Slice and Dice (swapped positions [from TBC])
  • Envenom (Eviscerate can activate poisons)
  • Crippling Brew (Instant Poison also applies Crippling Poison [from WOTLK])
  • Vigor (regains energy, requires 5/5 Seal Fate)
Combat
  • Improved Sprint (available earlier)
  • Endurance (-1 minute per point)
  • Deadened Nerves (passive defence, enables the Rogue to achieve 20% damage reduction if tanking with Blade Dance)
  • Dagger Specialization & Fist Weapon Specialization (merged [from WOTLK])
  • Blade Dance (tanking finishing move [from SOD], periodic AOE threat)
Subtlety
  • Master of Deception (reduces chance to be crit)
  • Sleight of Hand (removed; unpopular)
  • Initiative (available earlier)
  • Elusiveness (-1 minute per point)
  • Sadistic Smile (extra threat for bleed tanking)
  • Improved Sap (100% reliable [from TBC]; available earlier for low level dungeons)
  • Heightened Senses (available earlier)
  • Cheat Death ([from TBC]; requires a defensive ability to be active, so not just a freebie; helps mitigate randomness in avoidance tanking)
  • Hemorrhage (stronger bonus but fewer charges [from TBC], refreshes bleeds on hit to enable a bleed tank option; added extra rank)
  • Premeditation (increases damage of next attack on the target [from Shadowstep, TBC])
New Abilities (1/2)
  •  
    Corrosion Powder
    Reagent: Khadgar's Whisker.
    Creates the reagent for Corrosive Poison. One Khadgar's Whisker makes 3 Corrosion Powder.
    [Note: Corrosion Powder is also sometimes contained in Heavy Junkboxes.]
    [Rank 1, learned from trainer at level 42. Requires 235 Poison skill.]
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Poisons
    Poisons can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.
    The Crippling Poison (Rank 2) icon has been fixed [from TBC]. The Wound Poison icon has been changed (to the Gromsblood icon) to distinguish it from Crippling Poison [from TBC].
  •  
    Throw
    The poison applied to the offhand weapon also counts as being applied to any thrown weapons that the Rogue uses. The Throw ability benefits from all active poison-enhancing talents and bonuses that are affecting the Rogue.
  •  
    Wound Poison
    Wound Poison (Rank 1/2/3/4) now reduces healing taken by 66/90/126/162 per application (an increase of 20%).
New Abilities (2/2)
  •  
    Shiv
    Cast: instant. Cost: 20 energy.
    An instant offhand attack that deals 50% offhand weapon damage and applies the offhand poison to the target. (Cooldown: 10 seconds.)
    [Note: Does not award a combo point. Dual Wield Specialization increases the damage, 5/5 deals 100% offhand weapon damage.]
    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
  •  
    Corrosive Poison
    Reagents: Y.
    Each strike has a 30% chance of poisoning the enemy, reducing their armour by X for 10 seconds and causing a large amount of threat. Stacks up to 5 times.
    [Note: Does not stack with other armour reduction effects. Lasts 30 minutes and has unlimited charges (like Crippling Poison).]
    [Rank 1: X = 200, Y = (level 30),
     Rank 2: X = 300, Y =           (level 42),
     Rank 3: X = 400, Y =      (level 54),
     Rank 4: X = 450, Y = (level 60).
    Ranks 1-3 are learned from trainer; Rank 4 is learned from a bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
