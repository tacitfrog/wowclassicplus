Exploits
  •  
    NPC Camping
    Excessive interactions with an NPC vendor over time will apply a hidden debuff onto the player that will block him from seeing any limited-supply items in all NPCs' vendor windows for a period of time. The debuff will persist through logout and death and can last up to 30 minutes. It is intended to disadvantage campers in the race to buy limited-supply items (the camper will not know that he is blinded, so it will also disrupt time-based relog camping) and its duration scales proportionally with the aggressiveness of the camping over time.
  •  
    Slipping (Debuff)
    It is possible to jump to locations where you can attack other players but avoid retribution from players or guards - examples include the arena roof in Gadgetzan, the arch in Cenarion Hold, the bank in Stormwind, and the auction house in Orgrimmar. A new debuff is now applied to any player while present in these hazardous locations. This debuff causes the player to slip over every 3 seconds. Each slip knocks the player down for 1 second and causes damage equal to 10% of total health.

    Neutral guards will not attack players who initiate combat with players who have the Slipping debuff.

    Exploiting these locations to cause grief remains a bannable offence.

Magic Resistance
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance now increases all resistances by 40, up from 25.
  •  
    Tier 0 and 0.5 Sets
    The T0 8-piece and T0.5 2-piece set bonuses now increase all resistances by 16, up from 8.
  •  
    Prismcharm
    Prismcharm now increases all resistances by 40, up from 20.
  •  
    Racial Traits
    All racial traits that increase a magical resistance (Dwarf, Gnome, Night Elf, Tauren, and Undead) now increase it by 20, up from 10.
Items
  •  
    Scrolls
    All consumable scrolls that give a 30-minute stat buff now stack up to 10.
  •  
    Moist Towelette
    Moist Towelette is still a bind-on-pickup consumable that stacks up to 10. It is instant cast and now removes a single negative magic effect when used (instead of any type of negative effect). It cannot be used while feared, seduced, polymorphed, etc. It has a 1 minute cooldown that is not shared with anything else (similar to anti-venoms). In addition to a couple of old quests, new repeatable ways to acquire these items have been added via PVP honour quests and Full Moon event quests. They can also be obtained from the Darkmoon Faire.
