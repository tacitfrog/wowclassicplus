World Buffs
  •  
    Deep Delve (Debuff)
    World buffs no longer have any effect on players while inside any 40-man PvE raid dungeon. See under Endgame Raids.
  •  
    Chronoboon Displacer
    Chronoboon Displacer has been removed from the game.
  •  
    Locations
    Rallying Cry of the Dragonslayer now applies to faction players in all three of the faction's major cities, not just Stormwind and Orgrimmar.

    Spirit of Zandalar now applies to all players in Stranglethorn Vale (but not inside a Zul'Gurub instance), not just Yojamba Isle and Booty Bay.

Magic Resistance
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance now increases all resistances by 40, up from 25.
  •  
    Tier 0 and 0.5 Sets
    The T0 8-piece and T0.5 2-piece set bonuses now increase all resistances by 18, up from 8.
  •  
    Racial Traits
    All racial traits that increase a magical resistance (Dwarf, Gnome, Night Elf, Tauren, and Undead) now increase it by 20, up from 10.
Items
  •  
    Moist Towelette
    The item remains unchanged: it is still a bind-on-pickup consumable, removes a magic effect when used, has its own 1-second cooldown, and stacks up to 10. New repeatable ways to acquire these items have been added via PvP honour quests, Lord Kazzak hand-ins, and Full Moon event quests.
Miscellaneous Bugfixes
  •  
    The Hinterlands Inns
    The inns in The Hinterlands (at Aerie Peak and Revantusk Village) now count as inns and properly bestow the Rested buff.
