World Buffs
  •  
    Deep Delve (Debuff)
    World buffs no longer have any effect on players while inside any 40-man PvE raid dungeon. See under Endgame Raids.
  •  
    Chronoboon Displacer
    Chronoboon Displacer has been removed from the game.
  •  
    Locations
    Rallying Cry of the Dragonslayer now applies to all players in all three of the faction's major cities, not just Stormwind and Orgrimmar.
    Spirit of Zandalar now applies to all players in Stranglethorn Vale (but not inside a Zul'Gurub instance), not just Yojamba Isle and Booty Bay.
Dual Specs
  •  
    Second Talent Spec
    At level 40, each character gets a second talent spec. The character will then always have one active spec that is currently applied and one inactive spec that currently has no effect.
    Characters can switch between specs by using their Focus Lodestone.
    When a character first reaches level 40, the new spec appears as an empty inactive spec.
    When a character pays to unlearn and relearn talents, only the active spec is modified.
  •  
    Focus Lodestone
    Binds when picked up. Unique.
    A Focus Lodestone can be obtained for free by any character at level 40 or higher by talking to an appropriate class trainer. It can only be used while out of combat and under the effect of Rested (in a friendly inn or capital city); it has a 10 second cast and a 1 hour cooldown. When used, it switches the character's specs so that the inactive spec becomes active.
    [Note: This item is deliberately similar to a Hearthstone, but for talents.]
Magic Resistance
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance now increases all resistances by 40, up from 25.
  •  
    Tiers 0 and 0.5
    The T0 8-piece and T0.5 2-piece set bonuses now increase all resistances by 18, up from 8.
  •  
    Racial Traits
    All racial traits that increase a magical resistance (Dwarf, Gnome, Night Elf, Tauren, and Undead) now increase it by 20, up from 10.
