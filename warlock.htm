Primary Goals:
  • Sort out the Affliction tree; improve viability and uniqueness of heavy Affliction builds.
  • Add DOT minicrits, but not tied to crit chance (since Affliction is not a crit tree).
  • Add Seed of Corruption and provide some dispel protection for it (this was achieved by the Unstable Affliction ability in TBC).
  • Unlock heavy Destruction Fire builds for raiding by adding a non-specialised Fire damage spell (Incinerate).
  • Make Spellstones and Firestones viable by giving them unique properties via their talents.
Affliction
  • Improved Curse of Weakness & Improved Curse of Agony & Improved Curse of Exhaustion (merged; 5 ranks)
  • Improved Drain Life (2 ranks; bonus depends on other Affliction effects [from TBC]; does not count effects from other players)
  • Dark Pact (available for low level dungeons and builds; added 2 low level spell ranks)
  • Improved Drain Mana (3 ranks)
  • Paroxysm (gives Corruption and Drain Life a chance to minicrit)
  • Unstable Afflictions (gives some protection to magic debuffs from spam dispelling)
  • Seed of Corruption (AOE [from TBC])
Demonology
  • Improved Health Funnel (reduces health cost [from TBC])
  • Improved Demon Armor (gives more armour [from TBC] and health regeneration)
  • Unholy Power (includes Imp)
  • Improved Firestone (Fire damage that you deal can heal you [from Soul Leech, TBC])
  • Improved Enslave Demon (2 ranks [from TBC])
  • Improved Spellstone (improves mana management for both master and demon)
  • Soul Link (no longer requires Demonic Sacrifice, since they are not related)
Destruction
  • Improved Immolate (3 ranks [from WOTLK])

New Abilities
  •  
    Ritual of Souls
    Cast: 5 seconds. Cost: 80% of base mana. Reagent: Soul Shard.
    Begins a ritual that creates a Soulwell. Group members can click the Soulwell to acquire a Healthstone. The Soulwell lasts for 3 minutes or 10 charges. Requires the caster and 2 additional group members to complete the ritual. In order to participate, all players must be out of combat, right-click the soul portal, and not move until the ritual is complete. [From TBC.]
    [Note: Healthstones acquired from your Soulwell are your highest learned rank of Healthstone that the interacting player can use and benefit from any talent points that you have spent in Improved Healthstone.]
    [Rank 1, learned by completing a level 60 quest that is started by talking to a Violet Eye NPC in Swamp of Sorrows. This quest requires the Warlock to drain the soul of a particular demon boss in Karazhan Tower.]
  •  
    Incinerate
    Cast: 2.5 seconds. Range: 30 yards. Cost: 328 mana.
    Deals 306 to 368 Fire damage to your target and an additional 92 to 116 Fire damage if the target is affected by an Immolate spell.
    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
  •  
    Hellfire (Rank 4)
    Cast: channeled (15 seconds). Cost: 1385 mana.
    Ignites the area surrounding the caster, causing 233 Fire damage to himself and 233 Fire damage to all nearby enemies every 1 second.
    [Rank 4, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
