Endgame Raids
  •  
    Deep Delve (Debuff)
    A new debuff called Deep Delve is applied to all players while inside any 40-man PvE raid dungeon (Molten Core, Blackwing Lair, Ahn'Qiraj, Karazhan Crypts, and Naxxramas). This debuff represents the gloom and despair of the deepest, darkest places in Azeroth and nullifies the effects of all buffs that are not applied by players or consumables. In other words, World Buffs no longer have any effect in 40-man raid dungeons.
    [Note: This change is meant to retain the quintessentially Classic, over-the-top nature of World Buffs and to preserve them as a fun addition to the game, but to remove the toxicity associated with them (and to make raid content more challenging and easier to balance).]
  •  
    Power Creep Mitigation
    To reduce power creep, many items from all 40-man dungeons after Molten Core, especially the weapons and trinkets from these dungeons, have had their item levels reduced and their stats re-balanced accordingly.
    Naxxramas gear has been nerfed the hardest, although it remains the highest item level gear in the game. This is because Naxxramas gear was originally balanced so that it would remain relevant up to level ~65 in TBC, it was not balanced for long-term usage in Vanilla.
    Tiered set pieces have had their primary stats reduced to meet their new item levels. Tier 1 set pieces remain at item level 66. Tier 2/2.5/3 set items have had their item levels reduced from 76/76-88/86-92 to 72/72-82/80-86. These sets retain their powerful set bonuses and prestigious looks.
  •  
    Tier 2.5 Acquisition
    The Brood of Nozdormu reputation required to acquire T2.5AQ equipment has been increased. To obtain the boots and shoulders, players will need Friendly (up from Neutral). To obtain the helmet and pants, players will need Honoured (up from Friendly). And to obtain the chest, players will need Revered (up from Honoured).
Karazhan
  •  
    The Master's Key
    Each player must have The Master's Key in his keyring in order to access either of the Karazhan dungeons. To obtain it, players must complete a questline for the Violet Eye. This chain will send players to collect mana crystals from elite blue dragonkin in Azshara, then in Winterspring, then Mana Remnants in Dire Maul: West, and then Spectral Tutors in Scholomance. The player will then need to provide an Enchanted Thorium Bar for the Violet Eye to craft the key.
  •  
    The Violet Eye
    New faction, based in a small camp in Deadwind Pass. Players earn reputation by killing mobs/bosses in the two dungeons; reputation is required for exchanging gear tokens for gear. When the player is Friendly or above, the faction quartermaster will sell food and drinks. When Honoured or above, they will also sell Hyjal Nectar, Glowing Mantle, Mantle of the Violet Eye, and crafting recipes:
     Alchemy: Recipe: Elixir of Greater Frost Power (Honoured), Recipe: Major Rage Potion (Revered), Recipe: Transmute Elemental Earth (Exalted).
     Enchanting: Formula: Enchant Bracer - Health Regeneration (Honoured), Formula: Rugged Vellum (Revered).
     Leatherworking: Pattern: Enhancement Kit - Superior Potency (Revered).
    There is also one member of the faction living in a remote homestead on the shoreline in northeastern Swamp of Sorrows, who starts a new Warlock quest.
  •  
    Glowing Mantle
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 5 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 1 mana every 5 seconds. Requires Honoured with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Mantle of the Violet Eye
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 4 mana every 5 seconds. Requires Exalted with the Violet Eye.

    Karazhan Tower
    New 20-man raid dungeon. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ20. [From TBC.]
    Tomes: A new ability and a new rank for an existing ability for each class drop as bind-on-equip tomes from bosses in Karazhan Tower.
     Hunter: Kill Command, Raptor Strike (Rank 9).
     Mage: Molten Armor, Arcane Explosion (Rank 7).
     Rogue: Shiv, Corrosive Poison (Rank 4).
     Warlock: Incinerate, Hellfire (Rank 4).
    New bind-on-pickup Enchanting formulae drop rarely from bosses in Karazhan Tower: Formula: Enchant Gloves - Arcane Power, Holy Power, Nature Power, Parry, Superior Strength.
  •  
    Karazhan Crypts
    New 40-man raid dungeon. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ40.
    T2.5KC: A new 5-piece tier set for each class comes from Karazhan Crypts. Bosses drop class-based gear tokens (like AQ40). These sets have the same item level as T2.5. To differentiate, we call them T2.5AQ and T2.5KC. The T2.5KC sets cater to specs that are usually not provided for, such as rogue tank, shadow priest, and feral druid. T2.5KC set bonuses:
     Mage:
     3: All damage you take while channeling Evocation is reduced by 90%.
     5: Your Arcane Missiles critical hits invigorate you for 75-125 mana and add 40 spell damage to your next damaging spell (lasts 10 seconds).
     Rogue:
     3: When you suffer a critical hit, you gain Lurk in the Shadows which reduces the crit chance of the next three attacks that hit you by 100% (lasts 10 seconds; melee, ranged, and spell crits; cannot occur more than once per minute).
     5: When you dodge, you regain 5 energy (cannot occur more than once per second).
     Warlock:
     3: Your Corruption ticks have a 3% chance to make your next Incinerate instant cast.
     5: Reduces the mana cost of and threat generated by your Destruction spells by 5%.
Note: Everything else in this Classic+ project can be implemented with relatively minor tweaks. However, Karazhan Crypts would require a lot of development effort. In the event that this cannot be achieved, the T2.5KC set should instead be available as an alternative set from AQ40 using the same tokens and skins, but with different stat priorities and set bonuses.
