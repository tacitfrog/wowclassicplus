    Silk Towelette
    A Silk Towelette enables players to remove temporary effects that are applied to weapons such as Wizard Oils and Rogue poisons. The player simply uses a Silk Towelette on the applied weapon (in the same way as applying an effect); this has a cast time of 3 seconds, consumes the Silk Towelette, and removes the effect. It is made using 1 Bolt of Silk Cloth, 1 Heavy Leather, and 1 Fine Thread. Silk Towelettes are non-binding, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. The pattern requires 140 skill and is an uncommon, non-binding drop from level 30-34 elite Scarlet mobs outside the Scarlet Monastery and inside the Scarlet Graveyard and Armoury.
