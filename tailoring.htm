    Silk Towelette
    Tailors can now make silk towelettes, which enable players to remove temporary effects that are applied to weapons such as Wizard Oils and Rogue poisons. The player simply uses a Silk Towelette on the applied weapon (in the same way as applying an effect); this has a cast time of 3 seconds, consumes the Silk Towelette, and removes the effect.
    A Silk Towelette can be made using 1 Bolt of Silk Cloth, 1 Heavy Leather, and 1 Fine Thread. These can be mailed, traded, and auctioned and they stack up to 10. The pattern requires 140 skill, is bind-on-equip, and is an uncommon drop from level 30-34 Scarlet mobs outside the Scarlet Monastery and inside the Scarlet Graveyard and Armoury.
