Primary Goals:
  • Make heavy Arcane builds viable and functionally unique.
  • Fix the relationship between Arcane Concentration and Arcane Missiles.
  • Add a small radial heal component to Arcane spells for AOE synergy and supportive melee raid healing. [Arcane Volatility.]
Arcane
  • Arcane Concentration (chance to proc per missile with Arcane Missiles)
  • Arcane Subtlety (moved later)
  • Arcane Escalation (increases Arcane Missiles damage over time)
  • Improved Mana Shield (better value per point)
  • Improved Blink (reduces mana cost [from TBC])
  • Arcane Volatility (Arcane Missiles and Arcane Explosion crits heal nearby group members)
Fire
  • Fire Warding (renamed from Improved Fire Ward; includes Molten Armor)
  • Pyromania (spell crits reduce the cast time of Flamestrike [from WOTLK])
Frost
  • Elemental Precision (gives less hit [from TBC])
  • Improved Frost Nova (-3 seconds per point)
  • Arctic Winds (reduces chance to be hit [from TBC] and reduces mana cost of Frost Ward)
Modified Items
  •  
    Runes
    Rune of Teleportation and Rune of Portals now stack up to 20. [From TBC.]
  •  
    Illusionist Set
    The Illusionist (ZG set) 3-piece set bonus now decreases the mana cost of your Arcane Missiles and Arcane Explosion spells by 3%, instead of Arcane Intellect and Arcane Brilliance by 5%.
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Portals
    Portal spells cannot be cast in battlegrounds. See under Battlegrounds.
New Abilities
  •  
    Ritual of Refreshment
    Cast: 5 seconds. Cost: 80% of base mana. Reagents: 2 Arcane Powder.
    Begins a ritual that creates a refreshment table. Members of your raid or party can click the table to acquire 10 Cinnamon Rolls and, if they use mana, 10 bottles of Crystal Water. The table lasts for 3 minutes or 40 charges. Requires the caster and 2 additional group members to complete the ritual. In order to participate, all players must be out of combat, right-click the refreshment portal, and not move until the ritual is complete. [From TBC.]

    [Note: A player must be at least level 55 to interact with a refreshment table and cannot interact with one more than once per second.]

    [Rank 1, learned by completing a level 60 quest that is started by talking to Lorekeeper Lydros in the Dire Maul library, as long as the player has read from a Tome of Conjure Food VII and has completed the Arcane Refreshment quest. This quest requires the Mage to kill the Shade of Aran in Karazhan Tower.]

  •  
    Molten Armor
    Cast: instant. Cost: 500 mana.
    Causes 60 Fire damage to attackers when they hit you with any melee, ranged, or spell and increases your chance to get a critical strike with spells by 1%. Only one type of armour spell can be active on the Mage at any time. Lasts 30 minutes. [From TBC.]

    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]

  •  
    Arcane Explosion (Rank 7)
    Cast: instant. Cost: 410 mana.
    Causes an explosion of arcane magic around the caster, causing 261 to 286 Arcane damage to all targets within 10 yards.

    [Rank 7, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tome dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
