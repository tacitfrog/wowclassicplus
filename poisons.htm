    Spite Powder
    Reagent: Khadgar's Whisker.
    Creates the reagent for Spiteful Poison and Spitebombs. One Khadgar's Whisker makes 3 Spite Powder. Spite Powder is non-binding, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and stacks up to 20. Can sometimes be found in Sturdy and Heavy Junkboxes.

    [Rank 1, is learned from a bind-on-pickup handbook purchased from Ravenholdt at level 38 when Friendly. Requires 170 Poisons skill.]

    Spiteful Poison
    Reagents: Z.
    Requires Rogue. Use: Coats a weapon with poison that lasts for 30 minutes. Each strike has a 30% chance of poisoning the enemy which instantly inflicts X to Y Nature damage and causes a moderate amount of threat [+170]. Has unlimited charges. Spiteful Poisons are non-binding, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20.
