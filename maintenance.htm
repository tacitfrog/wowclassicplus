Bugfixes
  •  
    The Hinterlands Inns
    The inns in The Hinterlands (at Aerie Peak and Revantusk Village) now count as inns and properly bestow the Rested buff.
  •  
    Alchemy Effect Icons
    Many alchemy consumables gave a buff with an effect icon that differed from their item icon. Examples include Elixir of Ogre Strength, Elixir of Fortitude, and Major Troll's Blood Potion. These have been fixed so that the effect icon matches the item icon.
  •  
    Winter Veil Disguise Kit
    The mail that is sent to the player after completing A Smokywood Pastures' Thank You can now be deleted without error if the player already has a Winter Veil Disguise Kit in his inventory or bank.
Deshittification (a.k.a. removing the junk that was added to Classic despite "#NoChanges")
  •  
    Chronoboon Displacer
    The Chronoboon Displacer and its associated mechanics, which enabled everyone in a raid to have every World Buff, at full duration not reduced by travel, which made raids easy, enabled the gear-buying meta, fuelled the bot epidemic, led to the over-abundance of over-powered Tier 3 gear, and therefore trivialised and unbalanced every aspect of PVE and PVP, in short breaking the Classic experience, have been removed from the game. Good f**king riddance!
  •  
    Mailbox Inventory Closure
    The player's inventory is no longer closed automatically when leaving a mailbox, as this was never the behaviour in Vanilla.
  •  
    Enslave Demon
    The Warlock's Enslave Demon spell, which had been pointlessly renamed to Subjugate Demon, has been restored. The name and text of the associated talent has also been fixed.
  •  
    Succubus
    The Warlock's unnecessary incubus demon has been removed. The quest to learn how to summon this demon, which was lazily duplicated from the succubus quest and resulted in the player having two sets of rewards, has also been removed. The name, text, and icon of the associated talent, and the text in all talents and items that referenced the incubus, have also been fixed.
  •  
    Character Creation
    The character creation bug that had misspelled male as 'body type 1' and female as 'body type 2' has been fixed.
  •  
    Dungeon Art
    The polymorph spell that had changed female imagery into bowls of fruit has been dispelled.
  •  
    Login Screen
    The line of irrelevant options that were defacing the top edge of the character login screen have been removed.
  •  
    Social Button
    The pointless Social button, which was added to the side of the chat window despite there already being one on the option button panel, and which was added so sloppily that it didn't even match the colouration of the surrounding buttons, has been removed.
