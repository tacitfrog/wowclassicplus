    Lair
    Lord Kazzak is now instanced on a 5-day reset (just like Onyxia). The Tainted Scar in Blasted Lands is now twice the size, extending southward. Deep in the south, a wall of mountains shrouded in eerie purple smoke separates the southernmost part, where his lair is located. Players must enter via a small cave to the west of these mountains, within which there is a raid portal.
    The instanced area is a large valley surrounded by mountains and smoke. There are four mixed packs of Dreadlords, Felguard Elites, and Manahounds around the front edge that must be killed first (or they will agro during the fight). Lord Kazzak stomps around in the middle and is balanced to a similar difficulty as Onyxia.
  •  
    Attunement
    A questline begins in Nethergarde Keep/Stonard at level 58. The questgiver fears that a massive demon has entered through the Dark Portal. The player must obtain an item from Felguard Sentries and then another from Doomguard Minions in Dire Maul: North. With both items, the player must go to the Shadow Altar in the west of the Blasted Lands to summon and defeat a doomguard mini-boss. Take this demon's hoof to the questgiver to get a Ground Demon Hoof (green quality trinket). To enter Lord Kazzak's lair, the player must be wearing this trinket (or an upgraded one) or have it in his inventory.
  •  
    Ground Demon Hoof
    Trinket. Equip: +4 health per 5 seconds. Use: Create a shield around you that absorbs up to 480 Shadow damage. Lasts 20 seconds. (Cooldown: 5 minutes.)
  •  
    Loot
    Lord Kazzak always drops 1 Dark Heart (purple quality item), which begins a quest that allows the player to swap his Ground Demon Hoof for an upgraded version. There are two versions to choose from: Essence of the Dark Master or Essence of the Demon Lord.
    He also drops his usual epic loot, 1-7 Felcloth, 1-2 Hellfire Spineleaves (see under Herbalism), and 1-3 stacks of 1-3 Demonic Runes.
    Every player can also loot his corpse to get a Demonic Shard; this (white quality) item is bind-on-pickup, non-unique, and does not stack. Using the item starts a repeatable quest, handed-in to the attunement questgiver; the reward for each one handed-in is 99 silver, 100 Alliance/Horde reputation, and either 2 Demonic Runes or 3 Moist Towelettes.
  •  
    Essence of the Dark Master
    Trinket. Equip: +6 mana per 5 seconds. Equip: +6 health per 5 seconds. Use: Create a shield around you that absorbs up to 960 Shadow damage. Lasts 20 seconds. (Cooldown: 5 minutes.)
  •  
    Essence of the Demon Lord
    Trinket. Equip: +100 attack power when fighting Demons. Equip: +6 health per 5 seconds. Use: Create a shield around you that absorbs up to 960 Shadow damage. Lasts 20 seconds. (Cooldown: 5 minutes.)
