    Darkmoon Carnies
    Faire vendors that sell limited-supply items are too easily camped by safely parked characters. To increase the chance that players will find something special when they visit, new limited-supply opportunities have been spread around the faire. (Note that other methods have also been introduced to prevent NPC camping: see under NPCs.)

    Stamp Thunderhorn (food vendor) can now also occasionally sell cooked fish on a limited supply: 1-3 Mightfish Steak, Cooked Glossy Mightfish, Nightfin Soup, Poached Sunscale Salmon, Grilled Squid, Hot Smoked Bass, Baked Salmon, or Lobster Stew.

    Sylannia (drink vendor) can now also occasionally sell 1 Volatile Rum or 1-4 Hyjal Nectars on a limited supply.

    A new NPC has been added to the zoo area of the faire. He is a herbalist, trains Apprentice and Journeyman Herbalism, and sells Herb Pouches. He can also sell all of the same selection of limited-supply herbs as Lhara, on an independent timer. He also has one additional limited-supply item on his list: 1-3 Runn Tum Tubers.

    A new NPC has been added near the bodybuilder area of the faire. He is labelled as a 'Rock Collector' and sells Mining Picks. He can also occasionally sell 1-6 Rough, Coarse, Heavy, Solid, or Dense Stones, 1 Shadowgem, Lesser Moonstone, Moss Agate, Jade, Citrine, Aquamarine, Star Ruby, Large Opal, or Blue Sapphire, or 1-3 Silver, Iron, or Mithril Ore on a limited supply.

  •  
    Firework Launchers
    Some firework and cluster launchers have been added to the Darkmoon Faire that players can interact with. This adds another activity to the faire and a free, regular way for players to use their fireworks (such as those obtained during the Lunar Festival).
  •  
    Sayge's Blessings
    Sayge's Blessings are no longer free. To encourage more player interaction with the faire and its elements, the blessing dialogue now costs the player 1 Darkmoon Faire Prize Ticket to initiate.
  •  
    60 Tickets - Moist Towelettes
    Gelvas Grimegate now offers a new (repeatable) quest that allows 2 Moist Towelettes to be obtained for 60 Darkmoon Faire Prize Tickets.
