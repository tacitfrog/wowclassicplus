    Darkmoon Carnies
    The vendors that sell limited-supply items are too easily camped by safely parked, AFK characters. To counter this, new limited-supply opportunities have been spread around the faire.

    Stamp Thunderhorn (food vendor) now also occasionally sells 1-2 of 1-3 types of cooked fish on a limited supply: Mightfish Steak, Cooked Glossy Mightfish, Nightfin Soup, Poached Sunscale Salmon, Grilled Squid, Hot Smoked Bass, Baked Salmon, or Lobster Stew.

    Sylannia (drink vendor) now also occasionally sells 1 Volatile Rum or 1-4 Hyjal Nectars on a limited supply.

    A new NPC has been added to the zoo area of the faire. He is a herbalist, trains Apprentice and Journeyman Herbalism, and sells Herb Pouches. He also sells all of the same selection of limited-supply herbs as Lhara, on an independent timer. He also has one additional limited-supply item on his list: 1-3 Runn Tum Tubers.

    A new NPC has been added near the bodybuilder area of the faire. He is labelled as a 'rock collector' and sells Mining Picks. He also occasionally sells 1-4 Rough, Coarse, Heavy, Solid, or Dense Stones, 1-2 Silver Ore, or 1-2 Mithril Ore on a limited supply.

  •  
    Firework Launchers
    Some firework and cluster launchers have been added to the Darkmoon Faire that players can interact with. This adds another activity to the faire and a free, regular way for players to use their fireworks (such as those obtained during the Lunar Festival).
  •  
    Sayge's Blessings
    Sayge's Blessings are no longer free. To encourage more player interaction with the faire and its elements, the blessing dialogue now costs the player one Darkmoon Faire Prize Ticket to initiate.
