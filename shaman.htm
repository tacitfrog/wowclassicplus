Primary Goals:
  • Make Elemental DPS builds viable in PVE raids.
  • Add passive mana regeneration options. [Unrelenting Storm, Water Shield.]
  • Make Enhancement tanking builds viable to enable a Shaman to tank in 5-man dungeons.
  • Add mechanisms to grab AOE agro (to complement Chain Lightning pulls). [Epicentre, Fire Totems.]
Elemental
  • Elemental Devastation (available earlier [from WOTLK])
  • Improved Fire Totems (includes Searing Totem)
  • Lightning Overload (chance to cast a duplicate missile [from TBC])
  • Unrelenting Storm (passive mana regeneration [from TBC])
Enhancement
  • Guardian Totems (3 ranks)
  • Improved Lightning Shield (better value per point)
  • Two-handed Axes and Maces (skill persists)
  • Elemental Weapons (increased Flametongue and Frostbrand damage modifiers; available earlier)
  • Improved Weapon Totems (increased Flametongue Totem damage modifier)
  • Spirit Weapons (passive effect based on weapon enhancement)
  • Epicentre (Earth Shock and Lightning Shield AOE)
Restoration
  • Improved Chain Heal (increases healing on targets after the first)

Modified Abilities
  •  
    Weapon Enhancements
    The damage caused by Flametongue Weapon (Rank 6) has been increased from {37 to 112} to {40 to 124} and by Frostbrand Weapon (Rank 5) from 187 to 215 [from TBC].

    The slowing effect of Frostbrand Weapon has been increased from 25% to 30%.

Modified Abilities
  •  
    Fire Totems
    Threat generation for Searing and Magma Totems has been reworked. If the totem hits the target before the Shaman is on the target's threat list, then all of the threat it generates for its full duration is attributed to the totem. In this case, if the enemy kills the totem and the Shaman still is not on the threat list, then the Shaman will be added to the bottom of the threat list with a threat value of 1. If the totem hits the target and the Shaman is already on the threat list, then all of the totem's threat will be attributed to the Shaman. Threat generated by Fire Nova Totems is always attributed to the Shaman.
  •  
    Sentry Totem
    Sentry Totem now has Stealth. Its effective stealth level is {Shaman's level - 1}.

  •  
    Water Shield
    Cast: instant. Cost: 305 mana.
    The caster is surrounded by 3 globes of water, granting 15 mana every 5 seconds. When a spell, melee or ranged attack hits the caster, 142 mana is restored to the caster. This expends one water globe. Only one globe will activate every few seconds. Lasts 10 minutes. Only one elemental shield can be active on the Shaman at any one time.

    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60 bind-on-equip tablet dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
