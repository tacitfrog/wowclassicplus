Primary Goals:
  • Make Elemental DPS builds viable in PVE raids.
  • Add passive mana regeneration options. [Unrelenting Storm, Water Shield.]
  • Make Enhancement tanking builds viable to enable a Shaman to tank in 5-man dungeons.
  • Add mechanisms to grab AOE agro (to complement Chain Lightning pulls). [Epicentre, Magma Totem.]
Elemental
  • Elemental Devastation (available earlier [from WOTLK])
  • Improved Fire Totems (includes Searing Totem)
  • Unrelenting Storm (extra shield charges and mana regeneration)
  • Lightning Overload (chance to cast a duplicate missile [from TBC])
Enhancement
  • Guardian Totems (3 ranks)
  • Two-handed Axes and Maces (skill persists)
  • Elemental Weapons (increased Flametongue and Frostbrand damage modifiers)
  • Improved Weapon Totems (increased Flametongue Totem damage modifier)
  • Spirit Weapons (passive effect based on current weapon enhancement)
  • Epicentre (Earth Shock and Lightning Shield AOE)
Restoration
  • Improved Chain Heal (increases healing on targets after the first)
Modified Items
  •  
    Ten Storms Set
    The Ten Storms (T2 set) 3-piece set bonus now increases the amount healed by your Chain Heal spell to targets after the first by 20%, down from 30%.

    [Note: This stacks with the new talent Improved Chain Heal to a potential 40%.]

New Items
  •  
    Totem of the Keepers
    Relic (Totem). Binds when picked up.
    Equip: Reduces the threat generated by your weapon attacks by 5%.
    Requires level 47. Drops from Celebras the Cursed in Maraudon.
  •  
    Elixir of Nature Power
    Alchemists can brew a new potion that Shamans may find useful. See under Alchemy.
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Weapon Enhancements
    The damage caused by Flametongue Weapon (Rank 6) has been increased from {37 to 112} to {40 to 124} and by Frostbrand Weapon (Rank 5) from 187 to 215 [from TBC].

    The slowing effect of Frostbrand Weapon has been increased from 25% to 30%.

  •  
    Magma Totem
    All threat generated by a Magma Totem is attributed to the Shaman. After dealing damage, the Magma Totem will be placed at the bottom of the victim's threat list so that it can be attacked if no other targets are reachable.
  •  
    Sentry Totem
    Sentry Totem now has Stealth. Its effective stealth level is equal to the Shaman's level.
New Abilities
  •  
    Totemic Call
    Cast: instant. Range: 100 yards.
    Returns your totems to the earth. [From TBC, but without the exploitable mana refund.]

    [Rank 1, learned from a level 30, rare, non-binding tablet dropped by Razorfen Totemics, Death's Head Sages, and Death's Head Seers in Razorfen Kraul.]

  •  
    Water Shield
    Cast: instant. Cost: 305 mana.
    The caster is surrounded by 3 globes of water, granting 18 mana every 5 seconds, even while casting. When a spell, melee or ranged attack hits the caster, 182 mana is restored to the caster. This expends one water globe. Only one globe will activate every few seconds. Lasts 10 minutes. Only one elemental shield can be active on the Shaman at any one time. [From TBC, but balanced for level 60.]

    [Rank 1, learned from a level 60, non-binding tablet dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]

  •  
    Magma Totem (Rank 5)
    Cast: instant. Cost: 730 mana.
    Tools: Fire Totem.
    Summons a Magma Totem with 5 health at the feet of the caster for 20 seconds that causes 88 Fire damage to creatures within 8 yards every 2 seconds. All threat that it causes is attributed to the Shaman.

    [Rank 5, learned from a level 60, non-binding tablet dropped by bosses in Karazhan Tower.]
