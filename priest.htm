Primary Goals:
  • Make Shadow DPS builds viable for PVE raids by enhancing its unique functionality.
  • Fix Lightwell.
Discipline
  • Wand Specialization (better value per point)
  • Silent Resolve (Holy and Discipline only, also protects spells [from TBC])
  • Improved Inner Fire (available earlier, increases number of charges [from WOTLK])
  • Absolution (reduces mana cost of cleansing spells [from TBC])
  • Improved Mana Burn (-0.5 seconds per point [from TBC])
Holy
  • Holy Reach (includes Lightwell)
  • Lightwell (works like a strong, indestructible, short-ranged healing totem)
Shadow
  • Spirit Tap (does not require killing blow)
  • Improved Psychic Scream (-3 seconds per point)
  • Improved Fade (increases duration and reduces threat while active)
  • Shadow Reach (2 ranks [from TBC])
  • Shadow Weaving (triggers from and affects racial spells)
  • Improved Vampiric Embrace (added mana gain [from Vampiric Touch, TBC])
  • Mind Melt (increases crit chance of Mind Blast [from WOTLK] and redirects threat when it crits)
Modified Items
  •  
    Aegis of Preservation
    The cooldown of Aegis of Preservation has been reduced from 5 minutes to 3 minutes.
Modified Abilities
  •  
    Channeled Attacks
    Mind Flay no longer counts towards the maximum number of debuffs on the target. See under Endgame.
  •  
    Feedback
    The amount of mana burned by Feedback (Human spell) has been increased by 20%: Rank 1/2/3/4/5 now burns 22/42/65/92/126 of the attacker's mana.
  •  
    Shadowguard
    The damage caused by Shadowguard (Troll spell) orbs has been increased by 40%: Rank 1/2/3/4/5/6 now strike the attacker for 28/49/71/98/126/162 Shadow damage.
New Abilities
  •  
    Shadow Word: Death
    Cast: instant. Range: 30 yards.
    Cost: 348 mana.
    A word of dark binding that inflicts 402 to 458 Shadow damage on the target. If the target is not killed by Shadow Word: Death, the caster takes damage equal to the damage inflicted upon the target. (Requires Shadowform. Cooldown: 30 seconds.) [From TBC, but restricted to Shadowform and balanced for level 60.]
