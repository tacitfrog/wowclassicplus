    Enhancement Kits
    To give Leatherworkers an exclusive profession bonus, enhancement kits have been added.
    Enhancement kits are items similar to armour kits that can be created by Leatherworkers, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, they do not stack, and they can be applied to glove items. Only a Leatherworker can apply them and doing so will soulbind the gloves to the Leatherworker. Applying a kit will overwrite any other armour kit or enchantment on the gloves. The lower level patterns are learned from Leatherworking trainers, the Greater patterns are rare bind-on-equip drops from [unspecified] mobs, and the Superior patterns are bind-on-pickup and sold by the Karazhan Faction (Revered for Superior Potency and Exalted for Superior Dexterity).
    Enhancement Kit - Dexterity: increases your skill with all weapons by 1. Requires level 20 and Leatherworking 150.
    Enhancement Kit - Greater Dexterity: increases your skill with all weapons by 2. Requires level 40 and Leatherworking 225.
    Enhancement Kit - Superior Dexterity: increases your skill with all weapons by 3. Requires level 60 and Leatherworking 300.
    Enhancement Kit - Lesser Potency: increases damage and healing done by your magical spells and effects by up to 4. Requires Leatherworking 75.
    Enhancement Kit - Potency: increases damage and healing done by your magical spells and effects by up to 8. Requires level 20 and Leatherworking 150.
    Enhancement Kit - Greater Potency: increases damage and healing done by your magical spells and effects by up to 14. Requires level 40 and Leatherworking 225.
    Enhancement Kit - Superior Potency: increases damage and healing done by your magical spells and effects by up to 20. Requires level 60 and Leatherworking 300.

    Mining Sacks
    Leatherworkers can now make 16-, 20-, and 24-slot mining sacks that hold the Mining Pick, ores, bars, and gems. [From TBC.]
    The pattern for the 16-slot sack is a rare bind-on-equip drop from goblin miners across Strangelethorn Vale. It requires 225 skill to make and is made using 18 Thick Leather, 4 Cores of Earth, and 2 Heavy Silken Thread.
    The other two patterns are bind-on-pickup and sold by the Thorium Brotherhood when Honoured and Revered. The first requires 275 skill to make and is made using 20 Rugged Leather, 2 Enchanted Leather, 1 Cured Rugged Hide, and 2 Rune Thread. The second requires 300 skill to make and is made using 6 Core Leather, 4 Cured Rugged Hides, 1 Essence of Earth, and 4 Ironweb Spider Silk.

    Money Pouches
    Leatherworkers can now make Money Pouches, which count as coinboxes.
    A Money Pouch can hold up to 50 gold, requires 125 skill to make, is learned from Leatherworking trainers, and is made using 6 Medium Leather and 1 Fine Thread.
    Coinboxes are now required to trade coins. See under Coin Trading.

    Vellums
    Cured Hides now have an additional use: in the creation of vellums. See under Enchanting.
