    Enhancement Kits
    To give Leatherworkers an exclusive profession bonus, enhancement kits have been added.

    Enhancement kits are items similar to armour kits that can be created by Leatherworkers; they are bind-on-pickup and can only be applied to glove items. Applying a kit will overwrite any other armour kit or enchantment on the gloves and will soulbind the gloves to the Leatherworker.

    The lower level patterns are learned from Leatherworking trainers. The Greater patterns are rare bind-on-equip drops [Dexterity drops from Shadowforge Ambushers and Sharpshooters in Uldaman, Mageblood drops from Sandfury Witch Doctors and Shadowcasters in Zul'Farrak, and Potency drops from Celebrian Dryads in Maraudon]. The Superior patterns are bind-on-pickup and sold by reputation vendors [Dexterity is sold by Ravenholdt when Revered, Mageblood is sold by the Timbermaw when Revered, and Potency is sold by the Violet Eye when Revered].

    Mining Sacks
    Leatherworkers can now make 16-, 20-, and 24-slot mining sacks that hold the Mining Pick, ores, bars, and gems. [From TBC.]

    The pattern for the 16-slot sack is a rare bind-on-equip drop from goblin miners across Strangelethorn Vale. It requires 225 skill to make and is made using 18 Thick Leather, 4 Cores of Earth, and 2 Heavy Silken Thread.

    The other two patterns are bind-on-pickup and sold by the Thorium Brotherhood when Honoured and Revered. The first requires 275 skill to make and is made using 20 Rugged Leather, 2 Enchanted Leather, 1 Cured Rugged Hide, and 2 Rune Thread. The second requires 300 skill to make and is made using 6 Core Leather, 4 Cured Rugged Hides, 1 Essence of Earth, and 4 Ironweb Spider Silk.
