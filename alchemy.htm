•  
    Transmute Elemental Water
    Alchemists can now transmute a Globe of Water into 3 Elemental Water on a 10 minute cooldown, analogous to the Transmute Elemental Fire recipe from the Thorium Brotherhood. The recipe can be learned by talking to Duke Hydraxis if you are at least Revered with the Hydraxian Waterlords and have at least 300 skill.
  •  
    Transmute Elemental Earth
    Alchemists can now transmute a Core of Earth into 3 Elemental Earth on a 10 minute cooldown. The recipe requires 300 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Karazhan Faction.
  •  
    Oils
    Shadow Oil and Frost Oil can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance has been buffed. See under Magic Resistance.
