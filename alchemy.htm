•  
    Transmute: Iron to Gold
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting an Iron Bar into a Gold Bar has been decreased from 1 day to 1 hour.
  •  
    Transmute: Mithril to Truesilver
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting a Mithril Bar into a Truesilver Bar has been decreased from 2 days to 1 hour.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Fire
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting a Heart of Fire into 3 Elemental Fires has been increased from 10 minutes to 1 hour.
  •  
    Oils
    Shadow Oil and Frost Oil can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance has been buffed. See under Magic Resistance.
  •  
    Major Troll's Blood Potion
    Major Troll's Blood Potions now have an additional use: in Enchant Bracer - Health Regeneration. See under Enchanting.
  •  
    Dreamless Sleep Potion
    The effectiveness of these potions has been increased by 33%. Dreamless Sleep Potions now restore 1600 health and mana over 12 seconds, up from 1200. Greater Dreamless Sleep Potions now restore 2800, up from 2100.
  •  
    Elixir of Nature Power
    Requires Level 40. Use: Increases spell Nature damage by up to 40 for 30 minutes. (Cooldown: 3 seconds.) Requires 2 Purple Lotuses, 1 Golden Sansam, 1 Lightning Eel, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 250 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from Noxxion in Maraudon.

    This elixir has also been added to the list of possible items received in the Cenarion Circle Cache, awarded on completion of the Morrowgrain Research quest.

  •  
    Greater Holy Protection Potion
    Requires Level 48. Use: Absorbs 1950 to 3250 Holy damage. Lasts 1 hour. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Elemental Air, 1 Dreamfoil, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 290 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from Cursed Paladins in Hillsbrad Foothills.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Water
    Alchemists can now transmute a Globe of Water into 3 Elemental Waters on a 1 hour cooldown. The recipe can be learned by talking to Duke Hydraxis if you are Exalted with the Hydraxian Waterlords and have 300 skill.
  •  
    Flask of Replenishment
    Requires Level 50. Use: Regenerate 12 health and mana every 5 seconds for 2 hours. You can only have the effect of one flask at a time. This effect persists though death. Requires 30 Golden Sansams, 10 Mountain Silversages, 1 Black Lotus, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 300 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from a boss in Karazhan Crypts.

    The effects of this flask do not stack with Troll's Blood or Mageblood potions, but do stack with similar effects from other sources, such as foods and spells.

  •  
    Elixir of Greater Frost Power
    Requires Level 50. Use: Increases spell Frost damage by up to 40 for 30 minutes. (Cooldown: 3 seconds.) Requires 3 Icecaps, 1 Elemental Water, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 275 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Honoured.
  •  
    Major Rage Potion
    Requires Level 57 and Warrior. Use: Increases Rage by 50 to 80 and increases Strength by 70 for 20 seconds. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Sabertooth Fang (which is now a white quality item), 1 Gromsblood, 1 Golden Sansam, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 290 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Revered.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Earth
    Alchemists can now transmute a Core of Earth into 3 Elemental Earths on a 1 hour cooldown. The recipe requires 300 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Exalted.
  •  
    Hellfire Spineleaf
    Alchemists with 300 skill can take a Hellfire Spineleaf to Gorzeeki Wildeyes in Burning Steppes to learn how to create Blackened Urns. Gorzeeki will keep the herb but will permanently teach the recipe to the player. The recipe will be inserted under the Quest tab, along with the Gurubashi Mojo Madness recipe. See under Herbalism.
  •  
    Blackened Urn
    A Blackened Urn requires 1 Hellfire Spineleaf, 1 Ritual Candle (which can be found in Shadow Hold, Felwood), 1 Mysterious Rune (from Karazhan Crypts), and 3 Gromsbloods to make. They must be created on a Shadow Altar (new object, similar to an Alchemy Lab), which are sacrificial altars that can only be found in two places: in Shadow Hold and in a secret room in Karazhan Crypts. Blackened Urns can be mailed, traded, and auctioned and they stack up to 20. A Blackened Urn is required to summon Nightbane in Karazhan Tower and doing so will consume the item.
