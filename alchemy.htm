•  
    Transmute: Iron to Gold
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting an Iron Bar into a Gold Bar has been decreased from 1 day to 1 hour.
  •  
    Transmute: Mithril to Truesilver
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting a Mithril Bar into a Truesilver Bar has been decreased from 2 days to 1 hour.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Fire
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting a Heart of the Wild into 3 Elemental Fires has been increased from 10 minutes to 1 hour.
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance has been buffed. See under Magic Resistance.
  •  
    Oils
    Shadow Oil and Frost Oil can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.
  •  
    Elixir of Greater Frost Power
    Requires Level 50. Use: Increases spell Frost damage by up to 40 for 30 minutes. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 3 Icecaps, 1 Elemental Water, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 275 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Honoured.
  •  
    Major Rage Potion
    Requires Level 57 and Warrior. Use: Increases Rage by 50 to 80 and increases Strength by 70 for 20 seconds. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Sabertooth Fang (which is now a white quality item), 2 Gromsbloods, 1 Golden Sansam, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 290 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Revered.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Earth
    Alchemists can now transmute a Core of Earth into 3 Elemental Earths on a 1 hour cooldown. The recipe requires 300 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Exalted.
  •  
    Elixir of Greater Nature Power
    Requires Level 40. Use: Increases spell Nature damage by up to 40 for 30 minutes. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Purple Lotus, 2 Golden Sansams, 1 Heart of the Wild, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 250 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from Noxxion in Maraudon.
  •  
    Greater Holy Protection Potion
    Requires Level 48. Use: Absorbs 1950 to 3250 Holy damage. Lasts 1 hour. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Elemental Air, 1 Dreamfoil, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 290 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from Cursed Paladins in Hillsbrad Foothills.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Water
    Alchemists can now transmute a Globe of Water into 3 Elemental Waters on a 1 hour cooldown. The recipe can be learned by talking to Duke Hydraxis if you are Exalted with the Hydraxian Waterlords and have 300 skill.
