•  
    Transmute: Iron to Gold
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting an Iron Bar into a Gold Bar has been decreased from 1 day to 1 hour.
  •  
    Transmute: Mithril to Truesilver
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting a Mithril Bar into a Truesilver Bar has been decreased from 2 days to 1 hour.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Fire
    The transmutation cooldown triggered by transmuting a Heart of Fire into 3 Elemental Fires has been increased from 10 minutes to 1 hour.
  •  
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance
    Flask of Chromatic Resistance has been buffed. See under Magic Resistance.
  •  
    Oils
    Shadow Oil and Frost Oil can now be removed from a weapon by using a Silk Towelette. See under Tailoring.
  •  
    New Enchantments
    Major Troll's Blood Potions now have an additional use: in a new enchantment. See under Enchanting.
  •  
    Elixir of Nature Power
    Requires Level 40. Use: Increases spell Nature damage by up to 40 for 30 minutes. (Cooldown: 3 seconds.) Requires 2 Purple Lotuses, 1 Golden Sansam, 1 Heart of the Wild, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 250 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from Noxxion in Maraudon.
    This elixir has also been added to the list of possible items received in the Cenarion Circle Cache, awarded on completion of the Morrowgrain Research quest.
  •  
    Greater Holy Protection Potion
    Requires Level 48. Use: Absorbs 1950 to 3250 Holy damage. Lasts 1 hour. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Elemental Air, 1 Dreamfoil, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 290 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from Cursed Paladins in Hillsbrad Foothills.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Water
    Alchemists can now transmute a Globe of Water into 3 Elemental Waters on a 1 hour cooldown. The recipe can be learned by talking to Duke Hydraxis if you are Exalted with the Hydraxian Waterlords and have 300 skill.
  •  
    Flask of Regeneration
    Requires Level 50. Use: Regenerate 8 health and mana every 5 seconds for 2 hours. You can only have the effect of one flask at a time. This effect persists though death. Requires 30 Golden Sansams, 10 Mountain Silversages, 1 Black Lotus, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 300 skill and is an uncommon bind-on-equip drop from a boss in Karazhan Crypts.
  •  
    Elixir of Greater Frost Power
    Requires Level 50. Use: Increases spell Frost damage by up to 40 for 30 minutes. (Cooldown: 3 seconds.) Requires 3 Icecaps, 1 Elemental Water, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 275 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Honoured.
  •  
    Major Rage Potion
    Requires Level 57 and Warrior. Use: Increases Rage by 50 to 80 and increases Strength by 70 for 20 seconds. (Cooldown: 2 minutes.) Requires 1 Sabertooth Fang (which is now a white quality item), 1 Gromsblood, 2 Golden Sansams, and 1 Crystal Vial. The recipe requires 290 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Revered.
  •  
    Transmute: Elemental Earth
    Alchemists can now transmute a Core of Earth into 3 Elemental Earths on a 1 hour cooldown. The recipe requires 300 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by the Violet Eye when Exalted.
  •  
    Hellfire Spineleaf
    Alchemists with 300 skill can take a Hellfire Spineleaf to Gorzeeki Wildeyes in Burning Steppes to learn how to create Blackened Urns. The recipe will be inserted under the Quest tab, along with the Gurubashi Mojo Madness recipe. See under Herbalism.
  •  
    Blackened Urn
    A Blackened Urn requires 1 Hellfire Spineleaf, 1 Ritual Candle (which can be found in Shadow Hold, Felwood), 1 Mysterious Rune (from Karazhan Crypts), and 3 Gromsbloods to make. They must be created on a Shadow Altar (new object, just like an alchemy lab), which are sacrificial altars located in Shadow Hold and at the summoning circles in Burning Steppes and Blasted Lands. Blackened Urns can be mailed, traded, and auctioned and they do not stack. A Blackened Urn is required to summon Nightbane in Karazhan Tower and doing so will consume the item.
