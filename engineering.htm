    Salvaging
    Engineers can now gather from the corpses of mechanical mobs, such as Harvest Golems. The list of possible items obtainable from each type of mob includes a number of appropriate engineering materials and junk items, and sometimes multiple items are obtained. Requires {(mob level - 3) * 5} Engineering skill.

    Spitebombs
    Solid and Dense Blasting Powder now have an additional use: in the creation of Spitebombs. See under Rogue.
