    Coinboxes
    Engineers can now make Tin Moneyboxes and Steel Coinboxes, which count as coinboxes.
    A Tin Moneybox can hold up to 50 gold, requires 125 skill, is learned from Engineering trainers, and is made using 1 Tin Bar and 1 Bolt of Wool Cloth.
    A Steel Coinbox can hold up to 100 gold, requires 165 skill, is learned from a rare bind-on-equip schematic dropped from various mobs in Gnomeregan, and is made using 1 Steel Bar and 2 Bolts of Silk Cloth.
    Coinboxes are now required to trade coins. See under Coin Trading.
