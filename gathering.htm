    Herb Skinning
    Herbalists can now gather from the corpses of plant-based mobs, such as Tar Beasts and Warpwood Shredders. The list of possible items obtainable from each type of mob includes a number of theme-appropriate herbs and junk items, and sometimes multiple items are obtained. [From TBC.]
