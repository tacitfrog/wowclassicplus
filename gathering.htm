Herbalism
  •  
    Herb Bags
    Evergreen Pouch can now be placed in herb bags (since it is a herbalism tool, just like the Blood Scythe). [From TBC.]

    Windblossom Berries can now be placed in herb bags (just like Whipper Root Tubers and Night Dragon's Breath). [From TBC.]

  •  
    Herb Skinning
    Herbalists can now gather from the looted corpses of plant-based mobs, such as Fen Creepers. The list of possible items obtainable from each type of mob includes a number of appropriate herbs and junk items, and sometimes multiple items are obtained. Requires {(mob level - 3) * 5} Herbalism skill. [From TBC.]
  •  
    Hellfire Spineleaves
    A Hellfire Spineleaf is a new uncommon herb. These are non-binding, have no gold value, can be placed in herb bags, mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. They are dropped by Lord Kazzak and used to craft Blackened Urns (see under Alchemy).
  •  
    New Potions
    Purple Lotus, Sungrass, Gromsblood, Golden Sansam, Dreamfoil, Mountain Silversage, Icecap, and Black Lotus now have additional uses: in the creation of new consumables. See under Alchemy.
  •  
    New Food
    Ghost Mushroom and Mountain Silversage now have an additional use: in the creation of Ghost Broth. See under Cooking.
  •  
    Spite Powder
    Khadgar's Whisker now has an additional use: in the creation of Spite Powder. See under Poisons.
