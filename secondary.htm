    Shimmering Broth
    Requires level 45 and Rogue. Use: Restores 2148 health over 30 seconds. Must remain seated while eating. If you spend at least 10 seconds eating you will become well fed and gain 3% increased chance to dodge for 15 minutes. Requires 1 Mountain Silversage, 1 Soothing Spices, and 1 Hyjal Nectar (see under Karazhan Crypts) and is crafted on a cooking fire. Shimmering Broths are non-binding, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. The recipe requires 275 skill, is bind-on-pickup, and is sold by Ravenholdt when Honoured; it can only be purchased and learned by Rogues.
