The Violet Eye
  •  
    The Violet Eye
    New faction, based in a small camp in Deadwind Pass. Players earn reputation by killing mobs/bosses in the two dungeons or by handing in Mysterious Runes. When the player is Friendly or above, the faction quartermaster will sell food and drinks. They also sell Glowing Mantle, Infused Arrows and Bullets, Mantle of the Violet Eye, and crafting recipes:
     Blacksmithing: Plans: Nightmare Clasp (Honoured).
     Alchemy: Recipe: Elixir of Greater Frost Power (Honoured), Recipe: Major Rage Potion (Revered), Recipe: Transmute Elemental Earth (Exalted).
     Enchanting: Formula: Enchant Bracer - Health Regeneration (Honoured), Formula: Rugged Vellum (Revered).
     Leatherworking: Pattern: Enhancement Kit - Superior Potency (Revered).

    There is also one member of the faction living on an island in northeastern Swamp of Sorrows, who starts a new Warlock quest.

  •  
    Glowing Mantle
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 10 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 1 mana every 5 seconds.
    Requires level 55. Requires Honoured with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Infused Arrows/Bullets
    Ammo. Binds when picked up.
    Cost: 60 silver (per 200).
    Adds 15 damage per second.
    On ranged hit: drains 5 mana from the target and transfers it to you.
    Requires level 55. Requires Revered with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Mantle of the Violet Eye
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 4 mana every 5 seconds.
    Requires level 55. Requires Exalted with the Violet Eye.
Karazhan Tower
  •  
    Karazhan Tower
    New 20-man raid dungeon on a 3-day reset, using the assets from Karazhan in TBC. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ20. There are 4 mandatory bosses (Moroes, Curator, Chess event, and Prince Malchezaar) and 7 optional bosses (Attumen, Maiden, Opera event, Nightbane, Terestian Illhoof, Shade of Aran, and Netherspite). The Opera event spawns one random event per raid ID. A Blackened Urn is required to summon Nightbane (see under Alchemy).
  •  
    The Master's Key
    Each player must have the Master's Key in his keyring to access Karazhan Tower. To obtain it, the player must complete a questline for the Violet Eye that becomes available at level 55. This chain will send players to collect mana crystals from elite blue dragonkin in Azshara, then in Winterspring, then Mana Remnants in Dire Maul: West, then Spectral Tutors in Scholomance, and finally various mobs in Karazhan Crypts. The Violet Eye will then demand an Enchanted Thorium Bar and a Mysterious Rune to craft the key.
  •  
    Tier 1.5 Acquisition
    New Tier 1.5 pieces drop from bosses in Karazhan Tower and from Lord Kazzak:
     Head: dropped by Prince Malchezaar.
     Shoulder: dropped by The Curator.
     Chest: dropped by Lord Kazzak.
     Leg: dropped by Netherspite.
    Two class-specific pieces drop per boss kill.

    For the ring: class-specific bind-on-pickup gems drop from the Chess and Opera events (1 per event, random classes). The player will need the class-specific gem, a Nightmare Clasp, 3 Mysterious Runes, and Honoured with the Violet Eye in order to have the faction craft the ring for him. The Nightmare Clasp is made by a Blacksmith from an Infused Claw, 1-3 of which drop from Nightbane.

  •  
    Books
    A new ability and a new rank for an existing ability for each class can drop as bind-on-equip books from bosses in Karazhan Tower. One random book drops per boss kill, two from Prince Malchezaar and Nightbane, and none from the events (11 available per run). Requires level 60.
     Hunter: Kill Command, Raptor Strike (Rank 9).
     Mage: Molten Armor, Arcane Explosion (Rank 7).
     Rogue: Deadly Throw, Spiteful Poison (Rank 5).
     Shaman: Water Shield, Magma Totem (Rank 5).
     Warlock: Incinerate, Hellfire (Rank 4).

Karazhan Crypts
  •  
    Karazhan Crypts
    New 5-man dungeon, populated by Undead remnants of the Master's household and townsfolk, as well as some more dangerous elements. Similar difficulty and loot level to Scholomance. (This version is not based on the SOD version and assumes nothing from it.)
  •  
    Mysterious Runes
    Uncommon drop from mobs and bosses in Karazhan Crypts (with a similar drop rate to Dark Runes in Scholomance). These are not bound, have no usable effect or gold value, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. These are used as a reputation hand-in for the Violet Eye (100 reputation each), to create the Master's Key, to create T1.5 rings, to create Blackened Urns (see under Alchemy), to create Nightmare Clasps (see under Blacksmithing), and in a new enchantment (see under Enchanting).
  •  
    Hyjal Nectar
    Restores 4200 mana over 30 seconds. Must remain seated while drinking. Requires level 55.

    This high-level water only drops in Karazhan Crypts: it can drop from some mobs and can be found in some interactable barrels (similar to supply crates in Stratholme).

    It can also be purchased on a limited supply at the Darkmoon Faire.

  •  
    Recipe: Flask of Replenishment
    This bind-on-equip recipe drops from a boss in Karazhan Crypts. See under Alchemy.
Note: Everything else in this Classic+ project can be implemented with relatively minor tweaks. However, Karazhan Crypts would require a lot of development effort. If this cannot be achieved, Mysterious Runes should drop in Karazhan Tower (and not part of the key crafting) and the Alchemy recipe should be on the same loot table as Recipe: Flask of Petrification.
