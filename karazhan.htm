The Violet Eye
  •  
    The Violet Eye
    New faction, based in a small camp in Deadwind Pass. Players earn reputation by killing mobs/bosses in the two dungeons or by handing in Mysterious Runes. When the player is Friendly or above, the faction quartermaster will sell food and drinks. They also sell new bind-on-pickup items, including Glowing Mantle, Mysterious Arrows and Bullets, Mantle of the Violet Eye, and crafting recipes:
    • Alchemy: Recipe: Elixir of Greater Frost Power (Honoured), Recipe: Transmute Elemental Earth (Exalted).
    • Blacksmithing: Plans: Nightmare Clasp (Honoured).
    • Enchanting: Formula: Enchant Bracer - Health Regeneration (Honoured), Formula: Rugged Vellum (Revered).
    • Leatherworking: Pattern: Enhancement Kit - Superior Potency (Revered), Pattern: Cloak of Darkness (Exalted).

    There is also one member of the faction living on an island in northeastern Swamp of Sorrows, who starts a new Warlock quest.

  •  
    Glowing Mantle
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 10 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 2 mana every 5 seconds.
    Requires level 55 and Honoured.
  •  
    Mysterious Arrows/Bullets
    Ammo. Binds when picked up.
    Cost: 60 silver (per 200).
    Adds 15 damage per second.
    On ranged hit: drains 5 mana from the target and transfers it to you.
    Requires level 55 and Revered.
  •  
    Mantle of the Violet Eye
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 6 mana every 5 seconds.
    Requires level 55 and Exalted.
  •  
    The Frozen Eye
    Ring. Binds when picked up.
    Unique.
    +10 Stamina, +20 Frost Resistance. Requires level 60. When Exalted, the Violet Eye's jewelcrafter will offer a quest that rewards you this ring if you bring him some Glistening Sap from Winterspring, a Nightmare Clasp (see under Blacksmithing), 2 Mysterious Runes, and 4 Righteous Orbs.
Karazhan Tower
  •  
    Karazhan Tower
    New 20-man raid dungeon on a 3-day reset, using the assets from Karazhan in TBC. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ20. There are 4 mandatory bosses (Moroes, Curator, Chess event, and Prince Malchezaar) and 7 optional bosses (Attumen, Maiden, Opera event, Nightbane, Terestian Illhoof, Shade of Aran, and Netherspite). The Opera event spawns one random event per raid ID. A Blackened Urn (see under Alchemy) is required to summon Nightbane.
  •  
    The Master's Key
    Each player must have the Master's Key in his keyring to access Karazhan Tower. To obtain it, the player must complete a questline for the Violet Eye that becomes available at level 55. This chain will send players to collect mana crystals from elite blue dragonkin in Azshara, then in Winterspring, then Mana Remnants in Dire Maul: West, and finally various mobs in Karazhan Crypts. The Violet Eye will then demand an Enchanted Thorium Bar and a Mysterious Rune to craft the key.
  •  
    Tier 1.5 Set
    Some bosses in Karazhan Tower can drop new Tier 1.5 set pieces. See under Tier 1.5.
  •  
    Mission: Karazhan
    Ravenholdt Manor offers a new document-hunting quest. See under Ravenholdt.
  •  
    Ritual of Refreshment
    Mages have a new quest to learn the Ritual of Refreshment spell. See under Mage.
  •  
    Ritual of Souls
    Warlocks have a new quest to learn the Ritual of Souls spell. See under Warlock.
  •  
    Books
    New spells or spell ranks for each class can drop from bosses in Karazhan Tower as level 60 rare non-binding books (10% chance of 2 random books/40% chance of 1 random book dropping per boss kill, 20%/80% from Prince Malchezaar, none from events, for an average total of 6 books per full run):
    • Hunter: Guide: Hunter's Mark V, Guide: Raptor Strike IX.
    • Mage: Tome of Molten Armor.
    • Priest: Codex of Shadow Word: Death, Codex of Smite IX.
    • Rogue: Handbook of Deadly Throw, Handbook of Spiteful Poison V.
    • Shaman: Tablet of Water Shield, Tablet of Magma Totem V.
    • Warlock: Grimoire of Hellfire IV.
  •

  •  
    Fiery Warhorse's Reins
    Binds when picked up. Unique.
    Attumen the Huntsman's steed can drop extremely rarely from Attumen as a new very fast mount.
  •  
    Nightbane
    Nightbane can be summoned on the Master's Terrace inside Karazhan Tower; doing so requires a Blackened Urn (see under Alchemy), which is consumed. Infused Claws drop from Nightbane (1-3 per kill) and are used to craft Nightmare Clasps (see under Blacksmithing).
Karazhan Crypts
  •  
    Karazhan Crypts
    New 5-man dungeon, populated by Undead remnants of the Master's household and townsfolk, as well as some more sinister elements. Similar difficulty and loot level to Scholomance. (This version is not based on the SOD version and assumes nothing from it.)
  •  
    Mysterious Runes
    Uncommon drop from mobs and bosses in Karazhan Crypts (with a similar drop rate to Dark Runes in Scholomance). These are non-binding, have no usable effect or gold value, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. These are used as a reputation hand-in for the Violet Eye (100 reputation each), to create the Master's Key, to create T1.5 rings, to craft new items (see under Alchemy, Blacksmithing, and Leatherworking), and in a new enchantment (see under Enchanting).
  •  
    Hyjal Nectar
    Restores 4200 mana over 30 seconds. Must remain seated while drinking. Requires level 55.

    This high-level water only drops in Karazhan Crypts: it can drop from some mobs and can be found in some interactable barrels (similar to supply crates in Stratholme).

    It can also be purchased on a limited supply at the Darkmoon Faire and has an additional use: in the creation of Shimmering Broth (see under Cooking).

  •  
    Recipe: Flask of Replenishment
    This non-binding recipe drops from a boss in Karazhan Crypts. See under Alchemy.
Note: Everything else in this Classic+ project can be implemented with relatively minor tweaks. However, Karazhan Crypts would require a lot of development effort. If this cannot be achieved, Mysterious Runes and Hyjal Nectar should drop in Karazhan Tower (and the former would no longer be used for the key) and the Alchemy recipe should be on the same loot table as Recipe: Flask of Petrification.
