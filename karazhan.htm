The Violet Eye
  •  
    The Violet Eye
    New faction, based in a small camp in Deadwind Pass. Players earn reputation by killing mobs/bosses in the two dungeons; reputation is required for exchanging gear tokens for gear. When the player is Friendly or above, the faction quartermaster will sell food and drinks. They also sell Hyjal Nectar, Glowing Mantle, Infused Arrows and Bullets, Mantle of the Violet Eye, and crafting recipes:
     Alchemy: Recipe: Elixir of Greater Frost Power (Honoured), Recipe: Major Rage Potion (Revered), Recipe: Transmute Elemental Earth (Exalted).
     Enchanting: Formula: Enchant Bracer - Health Regeneration (Honoured), Formula: Rugged Vellum (Revered).
     Leatherworking: Pattern: Enhancement Kit - Superior Potency (Revered).
    There is also one member of the faction living in a remote homestead on the shoreline in northeastern Swamp of Sorrows, who starts a new Warlock quest.
  •  
    Hyjal Nectar
    Cost: 50 silver (per 5).
    Use: Restores 4200 mana over 30 seconds. Must remain seated while drinking. Requires level 55. Only becomes visible to purchase when Honoured with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Glowing Mantle
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 5 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 1 mana every 5 seconds. Requires Honoured with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Infused Arrows/Bullets
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 silver (per 200).
    Ammo. Adds 12.5 damage per second. On successful ranged attack, drain 6 mana from the target and transfer it to you. Requires Revered with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Mantle of the Violet Eye
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 4 mana every 5 seconds. Requires Exalted with the Violet Eye.
Karazhan Tower
  •  
    Karazhan Tower
    New 20-man raid dungeon. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ20. [From TBC.]
  •  
    The Master's Key
    Each player must have The Master's Key in his keyring to access either of the dungeons. To obtain it, players must complete a questline for the Violet Eye that becomes available at level 55. This chain will send players to collect mana crystals from elite blue dragonkin in Azshara, then in Winterspring, then Mana Remnants in Dire Maul: West, and then Spectral Tutors in Scholomance. The player will then need to provide an Enchanted Thorium Bar for the Violet Eye to craft the key.
  •  
    Tomes
    A new ability and a new rank for an existing ability for each class can drop as bind-on-equip tomes from bosses in Karazhan Tower. One random tome drops per boss kill.
     Hunter: Kill Command, Raptor Strike (Rank 9).
     Mage: Molten Armor, Arcane Explosion (Rank 7).
     Rogue: Shiv, Spiteful Poison (Rank 4).
     Warlock: Incinerate, Hellfire (Rank 4).
  •  
    Formulae
    New bind-on-pickup Enchanting formulae drop rarely from bosses in Karazhan Tower:
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Arcane Power,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Holy Power,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Nature Power,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Parry,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Superior Strength.
  •  
    Blackened Urn
    A Blackened Urn is required to summon the Nightbane boss. See under Alchemy.
Karazhan Crypts
  •  
    Karazhan Crypts
    New 40-man raid dungeon. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ40.
  •  
    Tier 2.5 KC
    A new 5-piece tier set for each class comes from Karazhan Crypts. Bosses drop class-based gear tokens (just like AQ40). These sets have the same item level as T2.5. To differentiate, we call them T2.5AQ and T2.5KC. The T2.5KC sets cater to specs that are usually not provided for, such as rogue tank, shadow priest, and feral druid. T2.5KC set bonuses:
     Hunter:
     3: Increases the damage done by your Kill Command by 10%.
     5: Rapid Fire also increases your melee attack speed and increases your critical strike chance with all attacks by 2%.
     Mage:
     3: All damage you take while channeling Evocation is reduced by 90%.
     5: Your Arcane Missiles critical hits invigorate you for 75-125 mana and add 40 spell damage to your next damaging spell (lasts 10 seconds).
     Rogue:
     3: When you suffer a critical hit, you gain Lurk in the Shadows which reduces the crit chance of the next three attacks that hit you by 100% (lasts 10 seconds; melee, ranged, and spell crits; cannot occur more than once per minute).
     5: When you dodge, you regain 5 energy (cannot occur more than once per second).
     Warlock:
     3: Your Corruption ticks have a 3% chance to make your next Incinerate instant cast.
     5: Reduces the mana cost of and threat generated by your Destruction spells by 5%.
Note: Everything else in this Classic+ project can be implemented with relatively minor tweaks. However, Karazhan Crypts would require a lot of development effort. In the event that this cannot be achieved, the T2.5KC set should instead be available as an alternative set from AQ40 using the same tokens and skins, but with different stat priorities and set bonuses.
