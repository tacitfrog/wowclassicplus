The Violet Eye
  •  
    The Violet Eye
    New faction, based in a small camp in Deadwind Pass. Players earn reputation by killing mobs/bosses in the two dungeons; reputation is required for exchanging gear tokens for gear. When the player is Friendly or above, the faction quartermaster will sell food and drinks. They also sell Glowing Mantle, Infused Arrows and Bullets, Mantle of the Violet Eye, and crafting recipes:
     Alchemy: Recipe: Elixir of Greater Frost Power (Honoured), Recipe: Major Rage Potion (Revered), Recipe: Transmute Elemental Earth (Exalted).
     Enchanting: Formula: Enchant Bracer - Health Regeneration (Honoured), Formula: Rugged Vellum (Revered).
     Leatherworking: Pattern: Enhancement Kit - Superior Potency (Revered).

    There is also one member of the faction living on an island in northeastern Swamp of Sorrows, who starts a new Warlock quest.

  •  
    Glowing Mantle
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 5 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 1 mana every 5 seconds. Requires Honoured with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Infused Arrows/Bullets
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 silver (per 200).
    Ammo. Adds 12.5 damage per second. On successful ranged attack, drains 5 mana from the target and transfers it to you. Requires Revered with the Violet Eye.
  •  
    Mantle of the Violet Eye
    Binds when picked up. Cost: 60 gold.
    Use: Permanently add to a shoulder slot item to restore 4 mana every 5 seconds. Requires Exalted with the Violet Eye.
Karazhan Tower
  •  
    Karazhan Tower
    New 20-man raid dungeon. Similar difficulty and loot level to AQ20. 4 mandatory bosses (Moroes, Curator, Chess event, and Prince Malchezaar) and 7 optional bosses (Attumen, Maiden, Opera event, Nightbane, Terestian Illhoof, Shade of Aran, and Netherspite) [from TBC]. The Opera event spawns one random event per raid ID. A Blackened Urn is required to summon Nightbane (see under Alchemy).
  •  
    The Master's Key
    Each player must have the Master's Key in his keyring to access Karazhan Tower. To obtain it, the player must complete a questline for the Violet Eye that becomes available at level 55. This chain will send players to collect mana crystals from elite blue dragonkin in Azshara, then in Winterspring, then Mana Remnants in Dire Maul: West, then Spectral Tutors in Scholomance, and finally various mobs in Karazhan Crypts. The Violet Eye will then demand an Enchanted Thorium Bar and a Mysterious Rune to craft the key.
  •  
    Tomes
    A new ability and a new rank for an existing ability for each class can drop as bind-on-equip tomes from bosses in Karazhan Tower. One random tome drops per boss kill, two from Prince Malchezaar and Netherspite, and none from the events (11 available per run).
     Hunter: Kill Command, Raptor Strike (Rank 9).
     Mage: Molten Armor, Arcane Explosion (Rank 7).
     Rogue: Deadly Throw, Spiteful Poison (Rank 4).
     Warlock: Incinerate, Hellfire (Rank 4).
  •  
    Formulae
    New bind-on-pickup Enchanting formulae drop rarely from bosses in Karazhan Tower:
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Arcane Power,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Holy Power,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Nature Power,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Parry,
     Formula: Enchant Gloves - Superior Strength.
  •  
    Tier 1.5 Set
    New 5-piece sets are available from Karazhan Tower. The sets have item levels 64-68 and contain the following pieces: head, shoulders, chest, legs, and ring.

    To acquire each piece, players will need a multi-class gear token that drops from bosses in Karazhan Tower (just like AQ40), Mysterious Runes from Karazhan Crypts, an Arcane Crystal, and a large amount of gold that is reduced depending on reputation level. The ring requires an additional item that only (but always) drops from Nightbane.

    The sets cater to specs that are usually not provided for, such as rogue tank, shadow priest, and feral druid. T1.5 set bonuses:
     Hunter:
     3: Increases the damage done by your Kill Command by 10%.
     5: Rapid Fire increases your critical strike chance with all attacks by 3% and also increases your melee attack speed.
     Mage:
     3: All damage you take while channeling Evocation is reduced by 90%.
     5: Your Arcane Missiles critical hits invigorate you for 75-125 mana and add 40 spell damage to your next damaging spell ('Arcane Invigoration' effect, lasts 10 seconds).
     Rogue:
     3: When you dodge, you regain 5 energy (cannot occur more than once per second).
     5: When you suffer a critical hit, you gain 'Lurk in the Shadows' which reduces the crit chance of the next 3 attacks that hit you by 100% (lasts 10 seconds; melee, ranged, and spell crits; cannot occur more than once per minute).
     Warlock:
     3: Increases the damage and reduces the mana cost of your Curse of Agony by 10%.
     5: Your Corruption ticks have a 3% chance to make your next Incinerate instant cast ('Shadow and Flame' effect, lasts 10 seconds).

Karazhan Crypts
  •  
    Karazhan Crypts
    New 5-man dungeon, populated by Undead remnants of the Master's household and townsfolk, as well as some more dangerous elements. Similar difficulty and loot level to Scholomance.
  •  
    Mysterious Runes
    Uncommon drop from mobs and bosses in Karazhan Crypts (with a similar drop rate to Dark Runes in Scholomance). These are not bound, can be vendored for 1 gold each, can be mailed, traded, and auctioned, and they stack up to 20. These are used to acquire Tier 1.5 set pieces, for the Master's Key, and in the creation of Blackened Urns (see under Alchemy).
  •  
    Hyjal Nectar
    Restores 4200 mana over 30 seconds. Must remain seated while drinking. Requires level 55.

    This high-level water only drops in Karazhan Crypts: it can drop from some mobs and can be found in some interactable barrels (similar to supply crates in Stratholme).

    It can also be purchased on a limited supply from the Darkmoon Fair [coming soon].

  •  
    Recipe: Flask of Replenishment
    This recipe drops from a boss in Karazhan Crypts. See under Alchemy.
Note: Everything else in this Classic+ project can be implemented with relatively minor tweaks. However, Karazhan Crypts would require a lot of development effort. If this cannot be achieved, Mysterious Runes and the Alchemy recipe should instead drop from Lord Kazzak.
